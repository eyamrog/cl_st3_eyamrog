With regard to evaluation, it is important to note that
this topic is currently undergoing updates in Cuba as well
as in other regions of Latin America. Several authors from
the region emphasize the necessity of reviewing evaluation practices in
preschool children, highlighting its significance in guiding the educational process
and preparing children for subsequent educational stages. Furthermore, these authors
describe evaluation in the preschool educational process as a reflective,
systematic, and rigorous process of inquiry that informs decision making.
