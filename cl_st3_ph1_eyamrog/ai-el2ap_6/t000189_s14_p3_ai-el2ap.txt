The electronic search was conducted in the following databases: PubMed
(Publisher Medline), Latin American and Caribbean Health Sciences Literature (LILACS),
and Scientific Electronic Library Online (SciELO). The search strategy utilized
the following descriptors: “Nutritional Deficiency,” “Vitamin A,” “Vitamin D,” “Zinc,”
“Iron,” “Anemia,” “Folate,” “Folic Acid,” “Respiratory Tract Infections,” “Influenza,” and
“Coronavirus.” These descriptors were identified using the Medical Subject Headings
(MeSH) from the U.S. National Library of Medicine (http://www.nlm.nih.gov/mesh/) and
the Health Sciences Descriptors list (http://decs.bvs.br).
