It is anticipated that the majority of the general population
will be infected with COVID-19 in the coming months, with
most individuals remaining asymptomatic or experiencing only mild symptoms. In
Brazil, the Ministry of Health identifies the following groups as
being at high risk for developing severe COVID-19: individuals over
60 years of age, those with severe cardiac or pulmonary
diseases, individuals with kidney diseases or diabetes, persons who are
immunosuppressed, and pregnant women.
