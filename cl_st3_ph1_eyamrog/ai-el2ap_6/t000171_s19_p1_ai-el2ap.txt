As of June 22, 2020, 638 cities had reported at
least one confirmed case of COVID-19, whereas 215 cities had
not reported any cases. Among the cities with reported cases,
it took 72 days from the first notification in the
state on March 4 for 319 cities—representing less than half
of the 853 municipalities in Minas Gerais—to register at least
one case of the disease.
