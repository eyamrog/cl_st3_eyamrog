In conclusion, leptospirosis cases were distributed across all 16 Health
Regions of Santa Catarina. Four regions—Northeast, Grande Florianópolis, Médio Vale
do Itajaí, and Foz do Rio Itajaí—accounted for 71.8% of
the total reported cases in the state. Additionally, the regions
of Northeast, Grande Florianópolis, Médio Vale do Itajaí, Foz do
Rio Itajaí, and Far West exhibited higher incidence rates of
leptospirosis compared to other regions.
