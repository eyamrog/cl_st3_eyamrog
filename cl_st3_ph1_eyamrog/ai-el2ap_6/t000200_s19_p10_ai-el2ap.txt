Among the eight Health Regions with the highest rates, MPV
decreased from 42.1% to 1.7% (a 96% reduction) when comparing
the period of higher rates and rainfall (October to March)
to the period of lower rates and rainfall (April to
September). In the eight regions with comparatively lower rates and
rainfall, MPV declined from 51.6% during October to March to
13.2% during April to September, representing a 74.4% decrease.
