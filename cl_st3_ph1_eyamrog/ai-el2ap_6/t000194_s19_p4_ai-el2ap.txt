The Perequê River exhibited 24 occurrences and two unique taxa:
Blodgettia indica Subram. (Figure 1a‑b) and Pyramidospora casuarinae Sv. Nilsson.
The first sampling recorded the highest number of occurrences (11),
while the second, third, and fourth samplings showed no significant
variation, with 4, 3, and 5 occurrences, respectively.
