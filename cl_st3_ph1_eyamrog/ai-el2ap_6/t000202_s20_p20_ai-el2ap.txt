Of the university students analyzed, 63% reported accessing an online
library within the last quarter, 14% within the past year,
6% more than a year ago, and 17% indicated that
they never or almost never accessed an online library. Regarding
frequency of online library use, 47% accessed it between one
and ten times, 19% between eleven and twenty times, 3%
between twenty-one and thirty times, and 6% between forty-one and
fifty times.
