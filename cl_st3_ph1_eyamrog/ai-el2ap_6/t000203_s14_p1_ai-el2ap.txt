Surveys of COVID-19 case numbers and deaths were conducted during
two periods: from December 31, 2019, to April 11, 2020,
and from April 11 to May 11, 2020. The countries
included in this survey were consistent with those reported in
April 2020, and for the purposes of this analysis, they
were categorized into eight groups based on specific characteristics described
