In both the first and second options, as shown in
Figure 1a and Figure 1b respectively, the duct discharges to
the exterior. Option 1 is in compliance with the guidelines
established by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC),
the American Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air-Conditioning Engineers (ASHRAE),
