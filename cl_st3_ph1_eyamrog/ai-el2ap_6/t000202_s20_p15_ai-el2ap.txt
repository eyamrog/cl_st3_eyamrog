The majority of students (96.8%) access the internet on a
daily basis. Their leisure activities online primarily involve viewing videos
and listening to music. Specifically, 86.3% of students consume videos,
with 50% using streaming services and 36.3% accessing videos through
unauthorized sources. In terms of music consumption, 85.2% of students
download music for free from the internet. Regarding book acquisition,
58.2% obtain books online, with 28.8% downloading them for free,
14.8% downloading them for a fee, and 14.6% purchasing them
through online retailers.
