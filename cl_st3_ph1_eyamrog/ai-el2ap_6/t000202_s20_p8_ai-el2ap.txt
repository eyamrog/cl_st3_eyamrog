The frequency of reading sports press, whether free or paid
and accessed online, is distributed as follows: 10% of respondents
read it daily, 8% read it only on weekends, 12%
read it at least once a week, 10% read it
at least once a month, 6% read it at least
once a quarter, 5% read it at least once a
year, and 12% report never or almost never reading it.
