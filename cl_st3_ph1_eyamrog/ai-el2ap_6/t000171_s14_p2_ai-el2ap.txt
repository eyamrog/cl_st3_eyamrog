The data utilized in this study were obtained from the
COVID-19 database provided by the Minas Gerais State Health Secretariat
(SES-MG) (7). The variables extracted include the incidence and mortality
rates of COVID-19 per 1,000 inhabitants between March 4, 2020,
and June 22, 2020, as well as the date of
the first reported case of the disease for municipalities that
had reported COVID-19 cases up to July 10, 2020.
