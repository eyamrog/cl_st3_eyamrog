Figure 3 presents the daily number of deaths in São
Paulo City over time. The data indicate a marked increase
in deaths up to April 5th, with an average growth
rate of 3.1 deaths per day. Consistent with trends observed
in the state population, the number of deaths stabilizes after
April 5th. However, unlike the state-level data, there is no
clear resurgence in the number of deaths after April 19th
in São Paulo City.
