In private offices, the limitation on dental and medical activities
to only urgent and emergency procedures presents a strong impact
on the economy of these sectors.76 This economic crises have
raised reflections and concerns that go beyond clinical security and
social detachment and have highlighted the importance of social security
and financial education. Such factors must also be taken into
account by the entities that guide dental practice, in order
to generate discussions to support the dentists on those occasions
where they will have to keep distance from their routine
clinical tasks during COVID-19.
