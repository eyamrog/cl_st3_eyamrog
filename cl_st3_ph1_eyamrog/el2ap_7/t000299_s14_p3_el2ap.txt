Were considered eligible studies related to pacifier disinfection, and published
from January/1999 to October/2019. No language restrictions were applied. Inclusion
criteria were: studies that analyzed different types of pacifier disinfection
independently from the method used, with at least a comparative
group. Exclusion criteria were: (1) addressed a topic other than
that of interest in this paper; (2) studies that only
observed pacifier contamination and /or biofilm formation; and (3) were
repeated studies.
