A treatment that improves the innate immune response will increase
the efficiency of the immune system as soon as the
virus enters the bloodstream. To this end, understanding the quality
of the individual or collective innate response is of paramount
importance. In order to achieve this, the authors have correlated
the epidemiological moment of the pandemic in different countries with
their BCG (bacillus Calmette–Guérin) vaccination status, which mainly acts by
activating or training innate immunity6.
