Conclusively, the ease and cost of doing business, availability of
infrastructural facilities amongst other incentives as regards the attraction of
investment flows in Turkey need to be given adequate and
due attention. This research recommends the government to reduce the
level of budget deficit owing/borrowing to improper management and improve
on financial discipline through accountability and transparency in resources allocation.
There is the need for the creation of friendly and
enabling environment for FDI to thrive in Turkey. By creating
enabling and stable environment that room for a stable interest
and inflation rate which in turn attracts foreign inflow of
investment.
