The covered hand removes the trocars in this “wrapped” manner,
while the other hand presses the plastic to completely seal
the incision site, protecting the professionals from possible spray of
secretions and aerosol, as well as from dissemination to the
room. The used disposable materials must be placed into usual
plastic disposal bags, closing it and twisting the neck of
the bag creating a knot, ensuring its sealing from the
outside environment.
