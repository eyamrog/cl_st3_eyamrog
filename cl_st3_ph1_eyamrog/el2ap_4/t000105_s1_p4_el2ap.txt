Results: We evaluated 1,762 residents, of which 764 (43.4%) were
in Mariana and 998 (56.6%) in Ouro Preto. For both
cities, 51.9% of the interviewees were female, with a predominance
of the age range 35 to 59 years old (47.2%).
The prevalence of the SARS-CoV-2 infection was 5.5% in all
cities, 6.2% in Ouro Preto, and 4.7% in Mariana. The
prevalence was similar between cities (P>0.05).
