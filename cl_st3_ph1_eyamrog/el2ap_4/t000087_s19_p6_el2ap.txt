In total there were 94 (57.3%) people that were unvaccinated.
28 (29.8%) had COVID-19 in the year before the event
and fully recovered before attending the event; none had a
reinfection due to the event. 83.7% reported not using a
face mask during the event. After the party, 33 (35.1%)
of the unvaccinated adolescents were COVID-19 positive; there were no
asymptomatic persons in this cohort. The most common symptoms were
fatigue, anosmia, general discomfort, runny nose, cough, low fever and
ageusia.
