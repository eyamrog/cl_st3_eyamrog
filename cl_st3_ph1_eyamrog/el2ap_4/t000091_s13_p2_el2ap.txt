Recently, the area of plant-based psychopharmacology has received much attention
from researchers. There is a variety of described mechanisms of
action of medicinal plants used to treat depression, anxiety and
insomnia, involving the reuptake of monoamines, which affects the activity
of the neuroreceptor and the transporter channel, modulating neuronal communication
or the hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal axis (Liu et al., 2015).
