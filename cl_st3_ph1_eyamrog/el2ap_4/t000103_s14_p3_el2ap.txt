A total of 181 people from 18 to 54 years
old (median of 20 years) have answered the survey. Almost
70% of the sample was female, and more than 90%
of them studied at a university. There were 78 Portuguese
and 73 Brazilian undergraduate students who were learning English (61
and 56, respectively) or Spanish (17 and 17) as a
foreign language, as well as 20 English and 10 Spanish
native speakers.
