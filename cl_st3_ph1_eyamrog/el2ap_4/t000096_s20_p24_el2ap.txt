The human being, as an active entity, must be prepared
for the changes that are frequently generated in a society.
Even more so, when the growth of information has been
exponential in its different forms of presentation (oral, written, multimedia,
audivisual,…), which requires information literacy as to be able to
search for it, select it, analyze it and transform it
into knowledge (Area & Guarro 2012), “basic competencies that are
part of the map of competencies needed to achieve the
goal of multi-literacy of citizens of the information society” (Martínez-Abad
2013:38).
