The first case of COVID-19 death in Brazil was documented
in the state of São Paulo on March 17, 2020.
From that date until May 15, 2020, 14,817 COVID-19 deaths
were confirmed across the country. São Paulo is one of
the states with the highest number of confirmed deaths due
to COVID-19 (4,501 deaths) and is the seventh among the
states with the highest death rates (98.02) per 1,000,000 inhabitants
(Figure 1). It is considered the epicenter of the disease
in Brazil, followed by Rio de Janeiro, Ceará, Pernambuco, and
Amazonas, which total almost 11,000 deaths.
