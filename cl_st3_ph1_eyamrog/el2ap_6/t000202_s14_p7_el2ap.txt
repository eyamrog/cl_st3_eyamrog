The applied survey is structured in three general modules (students'
identification data, cultural facilities and interest in cultural activities), and
twelve sectorial modules (museums, art galleries, archives, arts and craft
fairs, House of Culture, monuments, archaeological sites, reading and library,
ballet or dance, opera and theater, concerts of classical and
modern music, film and video, music, television, radio, computer and
Internet, use of free time practices related to leisure and
culture, and finally, a section dedicated to the cultural actions
and programs deployed by UNAE and to the participation and
interest of the student population.
