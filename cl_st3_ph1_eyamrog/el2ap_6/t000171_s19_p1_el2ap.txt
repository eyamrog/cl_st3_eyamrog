Until June 22, 2020, 638 cities had reported at least
one positive case for COVID- 19, while 215 cities had
not reported any cases of the disease. Among the cities
of the first group, 72 days (early May) have passed
since the date of the first notification in the state
- March 4 - for 319 cities (less than half
of the 853 municipalities in Minas Gerais) to register at
least one notification of the disease.
