Group of crucial importance in maintaining the trophic balance, Ganodermataceae
species play a key role in nutrient cycling of ecosystems,
causing white rot in woody tissues (Ryvarden 2004). The family
comprises species of recognized economic and medicinal value, and some
sources of bioactive compounds are widely studied for biotechnological purposes
(Zjawiony 2004, Tseng et al. 2008, Wang et al. 2016).
