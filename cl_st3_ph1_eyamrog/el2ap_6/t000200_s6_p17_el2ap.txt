In conclusion, leptospirosis focused in all the 16 Health Regions.
Four regions caught our attention (Northeast, Grande Florianópolis, Médio Vale
do Itajaí, Foz do Rio Itajaí), responsible in its entirety
for 71.8% of the cases in Santa Catarina. The Northeast,
Grande Florianópolis, Médio Vale do Itajaí, Foz do Rio Itajaí
and Far West regions presented higher risks of leptospirosis incidence.
