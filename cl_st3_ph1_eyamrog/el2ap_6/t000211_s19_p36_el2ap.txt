• Standardization of the professionals’ equipping sequence, in which surgeons
access a room, where they dress with PPE, including N95
mask, goggles, face shield, sterile waterproof suits, foot protectors and
two pairs of surgical gloves. Then, the surgeons go to
the operating room of the COVID-19 area, where an alcoholic
solution is applied to the second pair of gloves, a
sterile gown and an extra pair of sterile surgical gloves
are placed. After the surgery is completed, surgeons proceed to
a room located in front of the operating room, as
on a production line, to remove the PPE under the
supervision of a trained operator.
