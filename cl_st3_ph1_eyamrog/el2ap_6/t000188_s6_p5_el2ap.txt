In addition, the results of tests performed with the real-time
molecular test of the reverse polymerase chain reaction (RT-PCR) still
take more than 15 days to be released in many
places of the country. This factor leads to underreporting of
cases, excludes the diagnosis of asymptomatic people or those with
mild symptoms who do not seek health care or who
cannot access health institutions, and delays the early diagnosis of
cases with a higher risk of developing the severe form
of COVID-19.13,16
