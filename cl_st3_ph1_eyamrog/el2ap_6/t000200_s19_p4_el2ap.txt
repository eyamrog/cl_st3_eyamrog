The average number of cases of leptospirosis by rainfall rate
for Santa Catarina was 2.02 cases per mm of rain.
This number varied from 0.16 cases/PI in Serra Catarinense to
7.8 cases/PI in the Northeast region. Of the five regions
previously described as possessing the highest rates of leptospirosis, four
of them (Foz do Rio Itajaí, Médio Vale do Itajaí,
Grande Florianópolis, and the Northeast region) presented the highest numbers
of cases generated per unit of PI, representing 71.8% of
the total cases of leptospirosis generated throughout Santa Catarina, in
the period of study.
