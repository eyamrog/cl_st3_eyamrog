Almost all of the analyzed student population has a cell
phone (96%) and a high percentage has a smartphone (70%).
With respect to the cost of acquiring smart telephony, it
can be seen that it is females (14.4%) who make
the biggest investment in these devices, between 200-299 dollars, compared
to males (10.5%), which ranges from 100 to 199 dollars.
Regarding the monthly cost in cellular telephony, the range of
0 to 19 dollars predominates, both in males (35.2%) and
in females (25, 3%).
