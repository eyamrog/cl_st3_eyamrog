Fungal diversity - A total of 33 taxa of aquatic
Hyphomycetes were observed in water bodies of the Parque Estadual
da Ilha do Cardoso, with 97 occurrences (table 2). In
the first sampling, 18 taxa (26 occurrences) were recorded. The
2nd sampling presented 15 taxa and 3rd sampling 14 taxa,
and the numbers of occurrences were close (22 and 19,
respectively). On the 4th sampling, 17 taxa (30 occurrences) were
recorded.
