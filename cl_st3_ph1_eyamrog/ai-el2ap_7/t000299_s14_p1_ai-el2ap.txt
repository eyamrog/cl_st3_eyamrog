This review was conducted and reported in accordance with the
Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses (PRISMA) guidelines
[12]. The initial phase involved formulating a research question to
guide the study: "Is there a consensus on pacifier decontamination?"
The review protocol was completed and registered with the Prospective
International Registry of Systematic Reviews (PROSPERO) prior to the commencement
of the literature search, under the registration number 152011.
