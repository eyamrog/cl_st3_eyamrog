Of the eight studies included, seven were in vitro experimental
studies [3,8,9,11,13-15], and one was a cross-sectional study [10]. The
studies were conducted in Brazil (n=5) [3,8,10,13,15], South Africa (n=2)
[9,11], and India (n=1) [14]. Sample sizes varied, with three
studies including 10–50 participants or specimens [8,13,15], three studies including
51–100 [3,9,14], and two studies including 101–160 [10,11].
