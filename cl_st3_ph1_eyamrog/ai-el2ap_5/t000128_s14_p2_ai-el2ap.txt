This study utilized microorganisms isolated from the environment, including Staphylococcus
aureus, Micrococcus spp., and Corynebacterium spp. (LAMIV), as well as
standard reference strains: Staphylococcus aureus (ATCC 25923), Escherichia coli (ATCC
25922), Enterococcus faecalis (ATCC 29212), Pseudomonas aeruginosa (ATCC 27853), Streptococcus
agalactiae (ATCC 13813), Salmonella Typhimurium (ATCC 14028), and Klebsiella pneumoniae
(ATCC 700603).
