Regarding the distribution of reading types across the three academic
areas examined, syllabuses in the social sciences assigned the highest
number of journal articles (5,264) across the most syllabuses (457;
36.3%), followed by the humanities, which assigned 1,656 journal articles
on 165 syllabuses (23.9%), while the sciences assigned substantially fewer,
with only 1,041 journal articles on 86 syllabuses (5.9%) (Table
4). The social sciences also assigned a greater number of
scholarly book chapters and media articles than the other two
areas, both in relative and absolute terms. In contrast, humanities
syllabuses assigned the most trade-book chapters, with 570 chapters appearing
on 64 syllabuses (9.3%).
