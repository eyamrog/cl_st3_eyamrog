The sample comprised documents that simultaneously met all of the
following inclusion criteria: structured content based on epidemiological and health
indicators as defined by the World Health Organization (WHO); legal
validity during the data collection period; official status conferred by
the state level of government; a defined theoretical-conceptual and methodological
framework for economic reopening during the pandemic; a proposed agenda
for economic reopening; and public domain availability. Documents were excluded
if the full text was unavailable or if they originated
from unofficial sources.
