Figure 2 indicates that the University of Campinas, the Pontifical
Catholic University of Rio Grande do Sul, and Unesp have
data available for six out of the seven indicators, representing
the highest data coverage among the institutions analyzed. At the
lower end, the Rio de Janeiro State University has data
available for only three indicators, while the remaining 16 institutions
have data available for at least four indicators. In terms
of performance, Unesp (3 A, 1 B, 2 D) and
the University of Campinas (3 A, 1 B, 1 C,
1 D) achieved the highest grades in this dimension. The
State University of Maringá, the Federal University of São Carlos,
the Federal University of Pernambuco, and the University of São
Paulo follow, each with two A grades.
