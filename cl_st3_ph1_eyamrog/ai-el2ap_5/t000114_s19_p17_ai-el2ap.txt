Leaves incurved, oblong-lanceolate with denticulate margins; leaf cells papillose, each
bearing a papilla on the lumen. The species is corticolous,
rupicolous, and terricolous within the Atlantic Forest, and has been
recorded in the states of Minas Gerais, Rio de Janeiro,
Rio Grande do Sul, and Santa Catarina. It is considered
vulnerable in the Atlantic Forest and is distributed throughout Tropical
America.
