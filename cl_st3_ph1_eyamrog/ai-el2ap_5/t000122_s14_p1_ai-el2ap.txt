Specimens of Bostrychia binderi Harvey (BRASIL, PARAÍBA: Rio Tinto, Área
de Proteção Ambiental da Barra do Rio Mamanguape, s. d.,
Henrique Douglas dos Santos Borburema, JPBN° 63215) were collected from
the mangrove area of the Área de Proteção Ambiental da
Barra do Rio Mamanguape (6°46'15.00"S, 34°56'15.00"O) in February 2017. During
field collection, the majority of estuarine sediment was removed from
the specimens using in situ water.
