A total of 1,762 residents were evaluated, with 764 (43.4%)
from Mariana and 998 (56.6%) from Ouro Preto. Across both
cities, 51.9% of participants were female, and the majority were
aged 35 to 59 years (47.2%). The overall prevalence of
SARS-CoV-2 infection was 5.5%, with rates of 6.2% in Ouro
Preto and 4.7% in Mariana. There was no statistically significant
difference in prevalence between the two cities (P > 0.05).
