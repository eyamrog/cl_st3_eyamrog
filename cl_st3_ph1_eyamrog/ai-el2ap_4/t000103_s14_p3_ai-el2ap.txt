A total of 181 individuals aged 18 to 54 years
(median age = 20 years) participated in the survey. Approximately
70% of the sample were female, and over 90% were
university students. The sample included 78 Portuguese and 73 Brazilian
undergraduate students who were learning either English (61 Portuguese and
56 Brazilian) or Spanish (17 Portuguese and 17 Brazilian) as
a foreign language, in addition to 20 native English speakers
and 10 native Spanish speakers.
