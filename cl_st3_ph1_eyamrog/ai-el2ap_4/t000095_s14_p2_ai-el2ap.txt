All hospitalized adults (aged 18 years or older) diagnosed with
COVID-19 or unspecified severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS) between 1
January 2020 and 31 December 2020 were included in the
analysis. Patients with SARS attributed to other causes (including influenza,
other respiratory viruses, or other etiologic agents), as well as
individuals with Down syndrome and pregnant or postpartum women, were
excluded.
