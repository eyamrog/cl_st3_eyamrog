The analysis and discussion of the study protocols were conducted
by extracting data on the authors, year of publication, journal,
country of study, number of citations according to the Web
of Science database (as of February 7, 2021), number of
databases searched, search period, and adherence to the 27-item PRISMA
checklist (yes or no). Additionally, information was collected regarding the
study objectives, total sample size, intervention and control groups, participant
age, study location, variables investigated (both physical and psychological), specific
characteristics of the physical activity intervention, duration, number and frequency
of sessions, and overall duration of the intervention.
