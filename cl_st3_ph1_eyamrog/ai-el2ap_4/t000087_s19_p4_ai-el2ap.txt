Among the 41 adolescents diagnosed with COVID-19, 75.6% reported that
no secondary infections occurred among their close contacts, while 19.6%
indicated that they transmitted the infection to one or two
additional individuals. Regarding contact patterns, 73.2% stated that they had
no contact with other persons during their illness, whereas 7.3%
reported contact with three to four individuals, and another 7.3%
reported contact with five to six individuals. No hospitalizations or
deaths were reported among the adolescents or their close contacts.
