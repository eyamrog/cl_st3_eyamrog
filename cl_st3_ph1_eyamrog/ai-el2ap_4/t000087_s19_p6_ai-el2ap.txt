A total of 94 individuals (57.3%) in the cohort were
unvaccinated. Of these, 28 (29.8%) had previously contracted COVID-19 within
the year prior to the event and had fully recovered
before attending; none of these individuals experienced reinfection as a
result of the event. Additionally, 83.7% of participants reported not
using a face mask during the event. Following the event,
33 unvaccinated adolescents (35.1%) tested positive for COVID-19, and all
cases in this group were symptomatic. The most frequently reported
symptoms included fatigue, anosmia, general discomfort, rhinorrhea, cough, low-grade fever,
and ageusia.
