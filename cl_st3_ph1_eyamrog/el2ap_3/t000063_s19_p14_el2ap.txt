When asked the reason for wanting to study science (natural
sciences, math, statistics) or health sciences, the main reason selected
by those pursuing careers in these areas was “I like
that type of knowledge” in 39.7% of those intending to
study health sciences and in 48.4% of those wanting to
pursue natural sciences, math and statistics. 3.2% of those wanting
to study these careers reported that the ongoing pandemic had
inspired them to do so.
