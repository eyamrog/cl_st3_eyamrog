Study area – The Parque Estadual das Fontes do Ipiranga
(PEFI), located in the municipality of São Paulo, under the
coordinates are 23°39’S e 46°37’W at an average altitude of
798 m (Melhem et al. 1981) and the predominant phytophysiognomy
is the Dense Ombrophilous Rainforest. The sampling units were selected
near Lago das Garças, bordering a dirt road that connects
the Instituto de Botânica to the Zoológico de São Paulo
where there is a wide occurrence of H. coronarium (figure
1).
