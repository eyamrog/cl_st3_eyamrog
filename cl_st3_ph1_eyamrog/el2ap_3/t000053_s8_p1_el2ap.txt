A total of 22 articles/books, ranging from years 1964 until
2022, were used to obtain the data. As a result,
1,096 (two from preserved specimens) observations were recorded for the
state of Acre, records were obtained from eight municipalities, namely:
Acrelândia, Assis Brasil, Brasiléia, Bujari, Cruzeiro do Sul, Feijó, Rio
Branco and Xapuri. In these, 116 species of 15 genera
of sand flies were identified. Therefore, according to all studies
carried out on phlebotomine fauna, the state of Acre has
116 species reported.
