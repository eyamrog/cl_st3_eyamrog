According to the COVID-19 world map, presented in real time
by the Johns Hopkins Coronavirus Resource Center by the end
of the evening of May 31, 2020, 6,194,508 cases of
the disease and 372,501 deaths were confirmed worldwide. At that
date, the three countries with the highest number of confirmed
cases were the United States (1,790,191), Brazil (514,849), and Russia
(414,878). Those who lead the number of deaths were the
United States (104,383), the United Kingdom (38,571), and Italy (33,415),
