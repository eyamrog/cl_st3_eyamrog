The first case of COVID-19 was recorded in December 2019,
in the city of Wuhan, China [2]. In Brazil, the
first recorded cases were in February 2020 [3], and, among
the measures taken to contain the spread of the pandemic,
social distancing has been widely adopted as a non-pharmaceutical measure.
Other measures taken include the closure of educational institutions at
all levels, followed by restaurants, offices, and commerce in general,
in addition to others.
