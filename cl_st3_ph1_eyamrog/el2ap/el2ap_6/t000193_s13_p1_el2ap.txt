As social distancing becomes the recommendation to avoid COVID-19 infection,
new ways must be sought to achieve optimal management of
patients with chronic autoimmune disorders while still maintaining efficient disease
prevention. Recently, telemedicine has been adopted as a clinical management
tool, which now has legal support in our country (Law
no. 13,989 of April 15, 2020). Remote care has become
a safe way of monitoring patients during the pandemic.
