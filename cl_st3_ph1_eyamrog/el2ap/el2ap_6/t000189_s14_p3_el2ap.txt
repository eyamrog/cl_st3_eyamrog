The electronic search was performed in the databases: Publisher Medline
(PubMed), Latin American and Caribbean Health Sciences Literature Resource (LILACS)
and Scientific Electronic Library Online (SciELO). For the search strategy,
the following descriptors were used: “Nutritional Deficiency”, “Vitamin A”, “Vitamin
D”, “Zinc”, “Iron”, “Anemia”, “Folate”, “Folic Acid”, “Respiratory Tract Infections”,
“Influenza”, and “Coronavirus”. The descriptors were identified in the Medical
Subject Headings (Mesh), available at the U.S. National Library of
Medicine (http://www.nlm.nih.gov/mesh/), and in the list of Health Sciences Descriptors
(http://decs.bvs.br).
