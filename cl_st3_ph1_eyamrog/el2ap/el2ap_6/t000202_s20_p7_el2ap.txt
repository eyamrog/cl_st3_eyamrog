63% Of the analyzed university students are press or magazine
readers, distributed in 42% of women and 21% of men.
The frequency of reading general information press, both paid and
free on the Internet, is a daily 13%; 15% only
on weekends; 19% at least once a week; 9% at
least once a month; 4% at least once a quarter;
3% at least once a year; and 2% never or
almost never.
