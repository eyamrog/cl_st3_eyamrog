From the FCV-19S we could highlight two questions with significant
gender differences on their answers: the first (I am most
afraid of coronavirus-19), 74.1% of women agreed to be most
afraid of coronavirus-19, while 34.8% of men selected those answers.
The second question (It makes me uncomfortable to think about
coronavirus- 19), 66.2% of women agreed to felt uncomfortable thinking
about the coronavirus-19, while 26.1% of men opted for those
answers.
