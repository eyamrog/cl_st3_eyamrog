The coronavirus disease pandemic (Covid-19) emerged in Wuhan, China, in
late 2019; by the start of 2020, it had spread
to a dozen countries. Since then, the number of cases
has continued to escalate exponentially worldwide. Nowadays, Brazil is one
of the most aﬀected countries, as the disease has spread
to all five regions of the country – at the
start of the present study (19 June 2020), there were
1,038,568 conﬁrmed Covid-19 cases, including 49,090 deaths and 520,360 recoveries.
