The Perequê river presented 24 occurrences and two unique taxa,
Blodgettia indica Subram. (figure 1a‑b) and Pyramidospora casuarinae Sv. Nilsson.
The 1st sampling was with the largest number of occurrences
(11). Among the 2nd, 3rd and 4th samplings there was
no significant variation (04, 03 and 05, respectively).
