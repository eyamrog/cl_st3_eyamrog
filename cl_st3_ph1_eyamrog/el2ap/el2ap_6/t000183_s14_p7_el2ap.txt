Graphic Organizers for reading fall into the category of Graphic
Strategies. Although there is a large amount of them, the
most common ones are concept maps, mind maps, webs and
Venn diagrams. Based on previous studies and their practicality, the
following graphic organizers were chosen for the study: K-W-L chart
(know, want to know, learned); K-W-L-S chart (know, want, learned,
still); BRS chart (building and revising know schema chart; Sixty
second Skim and Scan; Cluster web; Concept web; Alphabet brainstorming;
Questioning cluster and B-D-A connection chart (before, during and after
