The expected duration of the pandemic is strongly related to
the development of new drugs and a possible vaccine [16].
It is not yet clear how long schools will be
closed as a measure to reduce or contain the contamination
resulting from COVID-19, or if there will be episodes of
intermittent opening and closing. This leads to a discussion of
the sustainability and effectiveness of initiatives carried out. With regard
to school feeding programs, the measures adopted need to be
adapted to these scenarios.
