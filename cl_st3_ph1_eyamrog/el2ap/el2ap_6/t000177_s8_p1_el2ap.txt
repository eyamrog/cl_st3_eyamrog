Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) was initially identified in an outbreak of
viral pneumonia in Wuhan, Republic of China, in December 2019.
The epidemiology of COVID-19 has recently become clearer as incident
cases continue to rise and researchers refine estimates of the
severity, transmissibility, and populations affected. The spread of Coronavirus is
causing in the society all around the world a considerable
degree of fear, worry and concern, with around one hundred
thousand people being infected worldwide daily [1, 2].
