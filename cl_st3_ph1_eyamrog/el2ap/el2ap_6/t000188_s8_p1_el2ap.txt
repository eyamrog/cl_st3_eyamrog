The world is facing a serious and acute public health
emergency due to the spread of the disease caused by
the coronavirus SARS-CoV-2, known as COVID-19. The World Health Organization
(WHO) declared on January 30, 2020, that the outbreak of
the disease was characterized as a Public Health Emergency of
International Importance, the highest alert level of the Organization, as
provided for in the International Health Regulations, beginning to be
considered, on March 11, 2020, as a pandemic.1
