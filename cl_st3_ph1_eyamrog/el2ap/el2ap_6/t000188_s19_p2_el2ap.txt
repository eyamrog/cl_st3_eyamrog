The state of Amazonas had the highest cumulative rate of
COVID-19 deaths per 1,000,000 inhabitants (321.14) during this period, followed
by the states of Ceará (161.63), Pernambuco (144.50), Rio de
Janeiro (141.21) and Pará (133.10). The lowest rates of cumulative
COVID-19 deaths per 1,000,000 inhabitants were reported by the states
of Mato Grosso do Sul (5.04), Minas Gerais (6.90), Mato
Grosso (7.46) and Goiás (9.55).
