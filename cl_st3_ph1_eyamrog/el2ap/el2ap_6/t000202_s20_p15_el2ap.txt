The majority of students (96.80%) connect to the internet daily.
The activities of leisure and free time made through this
media are reduced to viewing videos and listening to music.
86.3% consume videos, 50% through streaming service and 36.3% illegally
on the internet. Regarding music, 85.2% make downloads for free
over the Internet. And with respect to the acquisition of
books, 58.2% do so through the Internet, distributed in free
download (28.8%), download with payment (14.8%) and online shopping (14.6%).
