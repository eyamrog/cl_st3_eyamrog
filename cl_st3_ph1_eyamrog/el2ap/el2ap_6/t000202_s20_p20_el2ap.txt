63% of the analyzed university students accessed a library online
in the last quarter, 14% in the last year, 6%
more than a year ago and 17% never or almost
never. In terms of the number of times they access
a library online, 47% do it from one to ten
times; 19% between eleven and twenty times; 3% between twenty-one
to thirty, 6% between forty-one to fifty.
