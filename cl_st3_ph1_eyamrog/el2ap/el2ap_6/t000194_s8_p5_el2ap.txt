Several studies have expanded the knowledge of the geographical distribution
of anamorphic fungi in the Brazilian Atlantic rainforest (Schoenlein-Crusius &
Milanez 1989, Schoenlein-Crusius & Milanez 1990, Schoenlein-Crusius & Milanez 1998,
Grandi 1998, 1999, Gusmão et al. 2001, Grandi & Gusmão
2002, Silva et al. 2019). However, among them, only Silva
et al. (2019) included sampling points in a lentic system
on an island.
