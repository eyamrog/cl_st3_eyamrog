Renealmia petasites is very similar to R. chrysotricha Petersen, presenting
minimal differences, differentiating by the leaf blade wider 4-13 cm
wide, shorter calyx 1-2 cm long, bigger pedicel 0.5-1.5 cm
long, white labellum slightly pink and approximately 50 seeds per
fruit, while R. chrysotricha presents 4.5-9.5 cm wide leaf blade,
longest calyx 1.8-2.5 cm long, smaller pedicel 0.2- 0.6 cm
long, completely white labellum and up to 25 seeds per
fruit (Maas 1977, Lima et al. 2018)
