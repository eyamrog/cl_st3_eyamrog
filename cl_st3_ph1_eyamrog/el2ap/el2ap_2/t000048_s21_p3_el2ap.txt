Goeppertia monophylla is endemic in Brazil, found in the Atlantic
Forest in the States of Bahia, Espírito Santo, Minas Gerais,
Rio de Janeiro, São Paulo, Paraná, Santa Catarina and Rio
Grande do Sul (Saka et al. 2020). In the Núcleo
Curucutu, it is mainly observed in humid, shaded environments close
to watercourses.
