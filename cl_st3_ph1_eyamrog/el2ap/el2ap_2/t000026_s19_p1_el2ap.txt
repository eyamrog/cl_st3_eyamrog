Table 1 presents the characterization of the sample through the
descriptive analysis of sociodemographic and clinical data. Of the 286
research participants, the majority are female (77.9%), with a mean
age of 23.3 years (standard deviation SD= 4.9). They are
veterans (87.9%), enrolled in the Medicine course (44.2%), self- declared
white (49.8%), single (85.8%), attended private universities (80.4%), and mostly
lived in the state of Sergipe (58.2%).
