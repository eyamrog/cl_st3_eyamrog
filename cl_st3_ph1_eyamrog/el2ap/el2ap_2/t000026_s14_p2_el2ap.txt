The study included 286 university students from 13 different undergraduate
courses in biological and health science areas (biology, biomedicine, physical
education, nursing, pharmacy, physiotherapy, speech therapy, medicine, nutrition, dentistry, psychology,
chiropractics, and occupational therapy). The participants were selected by convenience
(nonprobability sampling) and by meeting the following criteria: (a) being
undergraduate in the health area in Brazilian Higher Education Institutions
(HEI), (b) being 18 years old or older, and (c)
having access to the internet.
