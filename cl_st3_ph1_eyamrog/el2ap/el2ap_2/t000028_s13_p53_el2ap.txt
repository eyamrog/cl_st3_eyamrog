Moving on, the following activities involve the vocabulary learned on
unit 4. There is an exercise reviewing School Subjects, which
asks students to write the corresponding subjects below the figures,
and another one reviewing the Days of the Week, which
requests students to write the seven days in order. Following
that, there is an activity to complete the family tree
with the Members of the Family, content learned on unit
5. In the following exercise, reviewing the vocabulary of unit
6, there are images of Parts of a House and
Furniture, below which students must write the referent words.
