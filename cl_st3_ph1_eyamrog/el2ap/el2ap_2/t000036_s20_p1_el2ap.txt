During the decade studied the region experienced the worst crisis
of the XX Century, the Great Depression. Although a variety
of symptoms of the crisis can be seen in the
Ecuadorian economic system, on the other hand, the GDP continued
to grow moderately between 1929 and 1934, at an average
of 1.5% (Morillo 1996:687), without registering decrease in none of
the years that the crisis lasted.
