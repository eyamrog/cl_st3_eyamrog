The average duration of edentulism identified in the sample of
the rehabilitated patients was 15.3 (± 15.3) years. Table 1
shows that tooth decay was reported as the cause of
tooth loss by all patients. Problems with eating and smiling
were the most cited problems faced due to missing teeth.
Among the reasons for not using dentures, financial factors appeared
most frequently. Finally, among the expectations regarding the dentures to
be made in the village, the most cited were an
expectation to improve one’s smile and to chew better.
