This Pension Fund included several benefits for workers such as
reimbursement for contributions, income for disability and work accidents, work-related
illnesses, medical assistance, retirement systems starting at age 75 or
with a total of 20 years of contributions, “if the
worker is unemployed or withdraws from the Fund for any
other legal reason, their contributions are returned intact” (Instituto Nacional
de Previsión 1939:7).
