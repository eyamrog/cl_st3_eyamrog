ABA farm is highly preserved area, with exotic species found
only in the vicinity of the residences. It has a
high expressiveness in species richness (322 species), besides presenting 38
new records. The area is classified by the MMA (Ministry
of the Environment) as an Caatinga area prone to desertification,
which makes it an area of high importance for conservation.
The data from this research will support the implementation of
an RPPN (Particular Reserve from the Natural Property) on the
farm and its management plan.
