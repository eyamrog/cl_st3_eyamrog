The Caatinga is located in the northeastern semiarid and occupies
an area of approximately 912,529 km² (Silva et al. 2017),
being one of the most distinct phytogeographic regions of the
Brazilian territory. Its flora, in the Caatinga domain, has a
representation of 5.022 species, with 2.307 species being found in
the Paraíba State, according to a study presented in BFG
(2021).
