Studied bromeliad - The species used were Aechmea distichantha Lem.
(figure 3 a) and Wittrockia cyathiformis (Vell.) Leme (figure 3
b). From 2013-2015, specimens of both species were rescued during
the removal of vegetation for constructing the northern section of
the Mario Covas Highway in São Paulo, São Paulo State,
Brazil. All of the bromeliads were in the adult stage.
