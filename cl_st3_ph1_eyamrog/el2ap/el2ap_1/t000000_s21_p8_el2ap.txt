Rhizomes long-creeping, with setose hairs. Leaves large, to 4.5 m
long; laminae 3-5-pinnate; veins free; indument formed by catenate hairs,
acicular hairs, arachnoid hairs, and/or minute gnarled hairs; sori marginal,
linear, continuous along the laminar margins, born on comissural veins;
inner indusia vestigial; outer indusia linear (Smith et al. 2006).
