It is worth highlighting the training characteristics of the participants.
In the Basic Group, most teachers had a master's degree
(n = 8), followed by two PhDs and one specialist.
The others had a background in social science (n =
3), pedagogy (n = 2), philosophy (n = 2), mathematics
(n = 2), geography (n = 2), arts (n =
1), chemistry (n = 1), history (n = 1) and
physical education (n = 1). Among the teachers in the
Technical Group, most had a Ph.D. (n = 9), seven
had a master's degree, and two were specialists. Their areas
of study were engineering (n = 10), followed by computer
science (n = 7), gastronomy (n = 2) and agribusiness
(n = 1). The Ethics Committee at the Institute of
Psychology of the Federal University of Bahia approved this research
project (CAA 92122318.1.0000.5686).
