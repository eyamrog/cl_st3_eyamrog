This movement towards high digitalization has been occurring more sharply
in Brazil since the end of the 1990s. We are
aware that this is not a natural course of events,
but rather, the result of political-economic choices reflecting the Brazilian
attitude to be part of a globalized world (Enders, 2004;
Akkari, 2011). These steps were further accelerated with the onset
of social distancing triggered by the Covid-19 pandemic, resulting in
the crisis dovetailing with a government project already underway.
