Rhizomes short-creeping, with primitive scales. Leaves small to medium-sized, ca.
20 cm to 1 m long; laminae simple to 3-pinnate;
indument absent or formed by 2-celled glandular hairs; veins generally
free; sori marginal or submarginal, elongate, born on comissural veins;
inner indusia modified, elongate; outer indusia not modified (Smith et
al. 2006).
