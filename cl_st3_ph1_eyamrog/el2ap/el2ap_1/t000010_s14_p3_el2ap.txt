Raven's Progressive Matrices Intelligence Test. It consists of 36 problems,
which are presented in a paper notebook. Each of them
consists of an incomplete figure that the child must complete.
It is a classic test to measure fluid intelligence. The
Cuban norms of Ramírez et al. (2017) were used. The
instrument shows adequate levels of reliability in the Cuban context.
The analysis indicated, 91% of the items with good discrimination
indices (items - total) (Ramírez et al., 2017).
