Music determines not only the speed, but also the characteristic
of the game to be played. For each rhythm, there
is a corresponding style of capoeira to be played. For
example, the rhythm called ‘Angola’ is played for a slow,
close game practised with one hand touching the ground, and
is an opportunity for the capoeirista to show off their
skills (ESPETO CAPOEIRA, 2022b). Sandro teaches his students to recognize
