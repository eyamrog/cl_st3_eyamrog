Identification. This species is characterized by having plicate peridium on
the internal and external surface, large peridioles (7-10 mm ×
6-7 mm) with bilayered cortex, dark brown to black peridioles
and basidiospores of 10-12 × 16-22 μm. Another distinctive feature
of the species is that at the base of the
pseudostipe a woolly golden brown subicule is formed (Brodie &
Dennis 1954, Trierveiler- Pereira & Baseia 2010).
