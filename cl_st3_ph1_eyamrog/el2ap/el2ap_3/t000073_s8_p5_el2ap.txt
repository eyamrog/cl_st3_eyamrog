Paraguay comprises two distinct geographical regions, the eastern and western,
which are separated by the Paraguay River. The western region
is also known as the Chaco. Which include 3 ecoregions:
Humid Chaco, Dry Chaco, and Pantanal. The ecoregions of eastern
Paraguay includes the Humid Chaco, Atlantic Forest, and Cerrado (Dinerstein
et al. 1995).
