Riparian forests in the study area usually have high plant
species diversity. Trees density and basal area are around, respectively,
1.300-1.900 individuals ha-1 and 32-38 m2 ha-1 (Nóbrega et al.
2001, Silva Júnior 2004, Silva Júnior 2005). Common species found
in these forests are Protium spruceanum (Benth.) Engl. (Burseraceae), Copaifera
langsdorffii Desf. (Fabaceae), Tapirira guianensis Aubl. (Anacardiaceae), Inga alba (Sw.)
Willd. (Fabaceae), Cheiloclinium cognatum (Miers) A.C.Sm. (Celastraceae), Pseudolmedia guaranitica Hasler
(Moraceae), Euterpe edulis Mart. (Arecaceae) and Talauma ovata A. St.-Hill.
(Magnoliaceae) (Nóbrega et al. 2001, Silva Júnior, 2004, Silva Júnior,
2005).
