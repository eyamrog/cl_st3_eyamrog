According to data from PNAD COVID-19, the percentage of employed
(and not on leave) people working remotely in the total
employed and not on leave population reached 13.3% in May,
decreasing to 12.7% in June, 11.7% in July and then
11.1% in August. In absolute numbers, the number of remote
workers in Brazil was 8.7 million in May and 8.4
million in August.
