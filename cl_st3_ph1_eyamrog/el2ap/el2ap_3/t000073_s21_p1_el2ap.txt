among 16 genera and four families. The most highly represented
family is Agaricaceae with 14 species (39%), followed by Phallaceae
and Geastraceae (with 10 species each respectively, 28%). Geastrum is
the genus with the highest number of species with nine
taxa (25%). The departments with the most records were the
