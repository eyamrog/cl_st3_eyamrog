Study área - We selected a riparian forest located in
Planaltina, Distrito Federal, Brazil. The Köppen- Geiger climatic classification is
Aw, with two markedly defined seasons (dry and wet) (Alvares
et al. 2013). Annual rainfall is 1,393 mm with nearly
100% of it falling in the wet season (October to
April) (Malaquias et al., 2010). Average temperature is 20.7°C. The
soil type is a clay Plintossolo (Plinthosols), according to the
Brazilian Soil Classification System (Embrapa, 2018).
