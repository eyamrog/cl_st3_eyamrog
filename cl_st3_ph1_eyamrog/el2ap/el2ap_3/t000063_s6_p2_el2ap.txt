The COVID-19 pandemic had an impact on the study intentions
in one fifth of the respondents (22%), being this impact
higher in those interested in health care (28%) followed by
those wanted to study a career in natural sciences, math
and statistics (23%). Most of the adolescents who wanted to
study health sciences and natural sciences stated that the reason
for selecting these careers was “I like that type of
knowledge” and only 3% of them reported that the pandemic
inspired their choice.
