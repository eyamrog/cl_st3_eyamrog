Identification. C. setosus is characterized by a conical peridium, of
intense dark brown coloration, smooth external surface and a slightly
plicated internal surface, with dark conspicuous setae 0.5-1 mm long,
large peridioles without a tunic, double cortex, and a small
subiculum (Brodie 1967, Trierveiler-Pereira & Baseia 2013).
