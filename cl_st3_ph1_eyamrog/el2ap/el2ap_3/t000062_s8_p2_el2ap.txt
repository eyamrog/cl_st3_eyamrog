Hedychium coronarium J. König (Zingiberaceae) known as the “White garland
- lily”, is a monocot invasive alien of great prominence.
It has herbaceous habit with competitive advantage over other species
due to its rapid growing and it´s phytochemical properties (Castro
2013). It has aromatic inflorescence that attract nocturnal pollinators (e.
g. moths) from January to March, H. coronarium has the
dispersion of its seeds carried out by birds (Ferreira et
al. 2016). It spreads asexually through rhizomes surpassing native vegetation
(Santos et al. 2005).
