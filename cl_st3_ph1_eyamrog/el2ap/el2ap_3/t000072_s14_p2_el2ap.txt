FAYS contains about 2608 hectare (ha) corresponding to the forest
fragments of lowland, brazilian savanna (cerrado), semidecidual forest, riparian forest
and forestry of Eucalyptus spp. and Pinus spp. The climate
is characterized as Cwa (Rolim et al. 2007), with hot
and rainy summers, while winters are dried. The average temperature
of the year 2018 was 21.90 ° C, while the
average annual pluviometry registered was 1291 mm (Universidade de São
Paulo 2020).
