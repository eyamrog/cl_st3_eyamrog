Also with participation of remote work above 40%, there were:
pedagogues and other teachers (languages, arts, music) and other professions
with higher education, such as lawyers, engineers, accountants, and journalists.
These categories fit well with the general "sector" in which
the hours of remote work are high, as defined by
Gaudecker et al. (2020), this being predominantly composed of personnel
with higher education and office occupations.
