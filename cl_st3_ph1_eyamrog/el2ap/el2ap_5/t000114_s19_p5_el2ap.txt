Reddish plants, leaves equally spaced, upper crowded, obovate to ovate,
serrulate margin toward the apex, bordered by 3-5 rows of
long cells, upper cells rombic and basals quadrate; costae percurrent
to excurrent. Corticolous, epixilous and terricolous in the Atlantic Forest
(lowlands to montane), recorded in the states of BA, MG
and RJ, throughout South America and Africa. In this work,
we prefer to use the accepted name for the species
according to Flora do Brasil 2020 (2021).
