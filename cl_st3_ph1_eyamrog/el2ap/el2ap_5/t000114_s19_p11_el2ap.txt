The most distinctive character is the costae excurrent, ridged dorsally
and in cross section with stereids in both of sides.
The identification of the species depends on the presence of
the sporophyte because it is distinguished from others by the
exothecial oval cells and peristome teeth with 2-3 teeth split
in 2/3. Our samples were found on rocks near to
the stream, in general occurs as terricolous in Atlantic Forest
(high montane), recorded in the states of BA, RJ, RS
and SP Endangered and endemic to Brazil.
