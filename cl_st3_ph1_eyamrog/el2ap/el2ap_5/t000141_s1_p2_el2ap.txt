OBJECTIVE: To analyze the content and characteristics of official documents,
which guided the period of transition and resumption of economic
activities in Brazil based on health indicators. METHODS: This is
a documental research, carried out between May and July 2020,
using official websites and publications from the state governments of
the 27 federative units in Brazil as sources. In the
study, only documents that used epidemiological and health indicators were
included as determining criteria for decision making in relation to
the easing, permanence or regression of social isolation measures adopted
during the COVID-19 pandemic.
