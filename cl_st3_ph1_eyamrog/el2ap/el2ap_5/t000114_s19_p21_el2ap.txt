Asymmetrics to falcates leaves when dry, stem leaves ovate or
oblong-lanceolate; branch leaves lanceolate, apex cuspidate; inflated alar cells and
2-3 rows of supra-alar cells. Our samples were found on
rocks exposed to the sun in an open environment, in
general, occurs as corticolous and epixilous in Atlantic Forest common
in high altitudes, recorded in the states of AM, BA,
ES, MG, PR, RJ, SC AND SP. Brazilian endemic.
