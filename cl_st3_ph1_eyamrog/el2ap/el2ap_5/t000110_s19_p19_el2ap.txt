It differs from Gomphonema hawaiiense Reichardt (2005: 119) by having
smaller valves dimensions (L: 24.5-34.0 µm; W: 4.5-6.0 µm and
L: 32.6-55.0 μm; W:6.3-9.5 μm respectively). (Tremarin et al. 2009)
and curved subcapitate apices. The taxon was found in 5%
of the core samples, reaching 1% maximum abundance. This species
is present in samples from the end of the Mid-Holocene
(~4.345 cal. yr BP). Current investigation represents the first report
of this species occurrence in the Brazilian Amazon.
