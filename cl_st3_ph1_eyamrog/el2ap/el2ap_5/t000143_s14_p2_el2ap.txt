in the country, has a geographical area of 21,926.908 km²
and a population of 2,068,017 people, being divided into 8
territories (Agreste Central, Alto Sertão, Baixo São Francisco, Centro Sul,
Grande Aracaju, Leste, Médio Sertão, Sul) and 75 municipalities, whose
greatest distance from the capital of the state is around
200 km.17. Sergipe is 20th in the national ranking of
the Human Development Index and 4th in the ranking in

for other municipalities in the state, it concentrates the best
