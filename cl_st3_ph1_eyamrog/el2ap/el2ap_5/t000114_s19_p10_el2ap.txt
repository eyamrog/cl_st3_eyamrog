Falcate leaves, lanceolate to linear, apex apiculate, with the upper
margin denticulate; long cells at margin and median portion of
the lamina. Our samples were found on rocks near to
the stream, in general occurs as terricolous in Atlantic Forest
(montane), recorded in states of GO, MG, PR, RJ, RS
and SP; Vulnerable and Brazilian endemic. This specie was not
in the Flora do Brasil 2020 (2021), so we used
the Dias, Peralta & Neto (2018) work to complement necessaries
information.
