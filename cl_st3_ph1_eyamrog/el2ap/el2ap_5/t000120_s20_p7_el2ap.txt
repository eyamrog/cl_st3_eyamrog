Species Diversity - including the two sampling periods, 27 species
were recorded (table 2), distributed across eight genera: Pacispora (one
species), Diversispora (one), Funneliformis (one), Archaeospora (two), Scutellospora (three), Dentiscutata
(four), Acaulospora (five) and Glomus (10), belonging to six families
(Acaulosporaceae, Archaeosporaceae, Dentiscutataceae, Diversisporaceae, Glomeraceae, Scutellosporaceae).
