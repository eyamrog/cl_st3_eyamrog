Microorganisms isolated from the environment (Staphylococcus aureus, Micrococcus spp., Corynebacterium
spp. – LAMIV) and standard strains (Staphylococcus aureus – American
Type Culture Collection – ATCC 25923, Escherichia coli – ATCC
25922, Enterococcus faecalis – ATCC 29212, Pseudomonas aeruginosa – ATCC
27853, Streptococcus agalactiae – ATCC 13813, Salmonella Typhimurium – ATCC
14028, Klebsiella pneumoniae – ATCC 700603) were used for this
research.
