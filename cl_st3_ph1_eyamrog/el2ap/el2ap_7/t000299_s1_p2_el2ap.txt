Material and Methods: The literature search was conducted on MEDLINE/PubMed,
Scielo, Lilacs, Web of Science, and Scopus databases to find
all relevant articles published over the past 20 years, based
on PRISMA guidelines. Two reviewers extracted data independently by using
a standardized form. The following factors were recorded: country of
study, type of study, pacifier material, sample number, microorganisms analyzed,
decontamination methods used, method accessibility and results found.
