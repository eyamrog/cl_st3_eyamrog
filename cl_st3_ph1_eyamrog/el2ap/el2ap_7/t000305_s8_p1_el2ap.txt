A series of pneumonia cases of unknown etiology appeared in
the city of Wuhan, in Hubei province, China, in December
2019, where the new coronavirus was identified and named as
2019-nCoV. Since this is a new strain of the virus,
and we lost the opportunity to isolate the first cases,
direct combat became very difficult. The first epidemiological information was
that the elderly with systemic atrial hypertension and diabetes mellitus
would be the main risk groups1.
