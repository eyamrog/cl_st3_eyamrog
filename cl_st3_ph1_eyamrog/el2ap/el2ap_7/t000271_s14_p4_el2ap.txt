Once they are accepted and donate blood, they are automatically
included in the study, provided they agree to sign the
informed consent for blood donation and for testing for other
pathogens not included in the mandatory list of infectious diseases
markers to be tested in all blood donations in Brazil.
Blood donation and sample collections were performed at a fixed
site – Hemorio central facility – or through mobile collections,
in churches and private condominiums, in the Rio de Janeiro
area.
