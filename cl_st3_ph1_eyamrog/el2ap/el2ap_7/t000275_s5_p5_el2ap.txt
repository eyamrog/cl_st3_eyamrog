Recent cases have shown that not only subclinical patients can
effectively transmit the virus but also, they can contaminate others
after recovering from the acute disease, depending on the viral
load17. These reports justify the need for PPE as well
as aggressive measures to guarantee safe handling to healthcare professionals
throughout the whole process. Every healthcare professional involved in the
treatment of patients in the COVID-19 pandemic era must follow
the strict guidelines of hand washing and PPE use.
