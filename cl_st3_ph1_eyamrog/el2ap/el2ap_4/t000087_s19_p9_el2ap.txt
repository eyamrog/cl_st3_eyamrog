A sample of one of the symptomatic fully vaccinated individuals
was sequenced using the extracted RNA material from the positive
qPCR test as described in our Methods. The result was
a genome with a 99.48% coverage assigned as the Delta
variant of concern (B.1.617.2) lineage according to the Pangolin COVID-19
Lineage Assigner website. Sequence was submitted to GISAID, Submission Number:
EPI_ISL_3982391
