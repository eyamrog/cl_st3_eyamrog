The crude death rate (Table 2) of hospitalized patients with
COVID-19 was greater in the state of Rio de Janeiro,
compared to São Paulo for all age-group and in both
private and public hospitals. From table 2, the crude risk
of mortality in the age group 18 to 40 years,
comparing Rio with São Paulo, was 2.9 in the private
hospitals and 2.1 in the public ones. These values for
the age group 90 years or more were 1.6 and
1.4, respectively.
