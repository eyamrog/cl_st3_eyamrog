In the Catálogo de Teses e Dissertações da CAPES website,
three searches were conducted with three different keywords and filters.
The first search was run with the keyword tradução (translation),
the filters arts, human sciences, language studies, translation studies, linguistic
studies, letters, classical letters, linguistics, linguistics and literature, medicine, and
medicine I. This search retrieved only one dissertation. The second
search used the keywords tradução médica (medical translation), the filters
language studies, letters, linguistics, and linguistics and arts and retrieved
four theses and two dissertations. Finally, the third search was
performed using the keywords tradução artigos científicos (translation scientific articles),
the filters human sciences, letters, letters and arts, linguistics, and
linguistics and literature and retrieved three theses and one dissertation.
