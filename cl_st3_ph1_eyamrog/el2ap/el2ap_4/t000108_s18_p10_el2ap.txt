Pleroma heteromallum has a wide distribution occurring in the southeastern
and northeastern regions of Brazil and less frequently in the
western center of the country (Guimarães 1997). In the State
of Minas Gerais, P. heteromallum is registered to 700- 1700
meters of elevation (Guimarães 1997). Pleroma tedescoi, on the other
hand, occurs only in Espírito Santo State, to 1700-1800 meters
