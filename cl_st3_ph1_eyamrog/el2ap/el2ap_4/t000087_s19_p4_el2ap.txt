Out of the 41 adolescents with COVID-19, 75.6% said no
one got sick around them (secondary infections), 19.6% said they
infected 1 or 2 more persons. 73.2% said they did
not have any contact with other persons, 7.3% had contact
with 3 to 4 persons, and 7.3% had contact with
5 to 6 persons. None of the adolescents or their
close contacts were hospitalized or died.
