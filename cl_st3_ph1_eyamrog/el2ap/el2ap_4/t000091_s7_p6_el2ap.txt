Phytoterapy has been widely used and generally accepted as established
treatment of depressive disorders, leading to the investigation of its
potential as a treatment form of various anxiety disorders (Kinrys
et al., 2009; Liu et al., 2015; Fajemiroye et al.,
2016). Phytotherapy is defined as the treatment of ailments using
fresh vegetables, plant drugs, or vegetable extracts prepared with these
type of raw material (Fajemiroye et al., 2016).
