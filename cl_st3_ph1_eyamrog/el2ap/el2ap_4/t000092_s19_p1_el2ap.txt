68 patients were studied. 62% were men and 38% women.
The mean age was 48.9 ± 13.3 years. The median
systolic blood pressure (SBP) was 120 mmHg (95 to 170)
and the diastolic blood pressure (DBP) 80 mmHg (60 -110).
The heart rate was 76 ± 13 beats per minute
and the respiratory rate was 24 (16 to 40). Anosmia
was observed in 22% (n = 15) and dysgeusia in
19% (n = 13).
