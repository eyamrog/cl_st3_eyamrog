All athletes responded in the locker room to the PMSL
(Engelmann, 1986) in three conditions: about 40 minutes before the
last training session preceding the match (Training- Previous condition), about
40 minutes before the match (Match condition) and about 40
minutes before the first training after the match (Training-Post condition).
Data were collected during three consecutive championship rounds, for a
total of three Training- Previous condition (TR-PRE), three Matches condition
(MATCH) and three Training-Post condition (TR-POST) assessments.
