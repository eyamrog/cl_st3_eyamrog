Renealmia petasites is endemic in Brazil, with occurrences in the
Atlantic Forest, distributed in the States of Espírito Santo, Minas
Gerais, Rio de Janeiro, São Paulo, Paraná and Santa Catarina
(André 2020c). Its occurrence in Bahia was also recorded (according
to: L.A. Mattos Silva 3673, M.M.M. Lopes 312 and D.
Cardoso 1668).
