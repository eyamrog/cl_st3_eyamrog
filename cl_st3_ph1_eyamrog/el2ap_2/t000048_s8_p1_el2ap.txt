Zingiberales is an order of tropical plants within monocotyledons formed
by eight families: Cannaceae Juss., represented by one genus and
10 species; Costaceae Nakai, which has six genera and 143
species; Heliconiaceae Nakai, one genus and 200 species; Lowiaceae Ridl.,
one genus and 20 species; Marantaceae R. Br., 31 genera
and 550 species; Musaceae Juss., three genera and 91 species,
Strelitziaceae Hutch., three genera and seven species; and Zingiberaceae Martinov,
56 genera and 1.600 species, totaling 102 genera and 2.621
species (Stevens 2017).
