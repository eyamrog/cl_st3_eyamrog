Study area - The municipality of Passagem is located in
the mesoregion of the Paraiba hinterland, bordering with Quixaba and
São Mamede by north, Taperoá e Cacimba by south, and
Cacimba de Areia by east (IBGE 2019) (figure 1). The
climate is semi-arid (Bsh), warm and dry, with an average
annual temperature of 25ºC (Francisco 2015, IBGE 2019).
