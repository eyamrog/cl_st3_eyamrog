By directing a look at health actions, both Oliveira, Martins
and Bracht (2015a) and Lopes, Nogueira and Rocha (2018), verified
the need for training actions and specific training of school
agents to work with this theme. According to Bagnara and
Fensterseifer (2019), PE teachers end up inserting themselves in teaching
without knowing the intentions of the curricular component, thus showing
the relevance of conducting studies on the theme, allowing the
propagation and dissemination of an expanded view of health.
