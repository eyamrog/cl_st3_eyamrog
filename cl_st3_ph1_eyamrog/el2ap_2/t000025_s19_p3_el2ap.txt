The selected studies presented the following characteristics: 54 (88.6%) were
published in English, four (5.7%) in Portuguese, and four (5.7%)
in Spanish. England was the country with the largest number
of publications (18 / 24.3%), followed by the USA (16
/ 22.9%). Twenty-two studies (31.4%) were published in the last
seven years (2015-2022). Nurses were the professional category that most
published on the topic (54 / 77.1%), followed by physicians
(15 / 24.1%) and pharmacists (1 / 1.4%).
