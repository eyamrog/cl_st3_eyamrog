In the State of Sergipe, which makes up most of
the sample, the government decree showing the public health emergency
situation was issued on March 17. For the Southeast region,
which concentrated the second largest part of the sample, the
state of São Paulo decreed it on March 20, the
state of Minas Gerais on March 15, the state of
Rio de Janeiro on March 16, and the state of
Espírito Santo, on March 17. For the other regions, dates
varied between March 16 and 19.
