Collection and maintenance of cultures - Specimens of Bostrychia binderi
Harvey (BRASIL. PARAÍBA: Rio Tinto, Área de Proteção Ambiental da
Barra do Rio Mamanguape, s. d., Henrique Douglas dos Santos
Borburema, JPBN° 63215) were collected in the mangrove of the
“Área de Proteção Ambiental da Barra do Rio Mamanguape” (6°46'15.00"S
and 34°56'15.00"O) in February 2017. During field collection, most of
the estuarine sediment was removed with in situ water.
