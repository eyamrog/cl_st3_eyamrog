Agency theory addresses the conflicts between principal and agent, and,
in our study, we brought its assumptions and concepts to
the third sector environment, as did other studies, for example
Ho & Huang (2017), Blevins, Ragozzino & Eckardt (2020) and
Balsam, Harris & Saxton (2020). Here we examine the agency
relationship observed in hiring an NPO (agent) by a donor
organization (principal), for the transfer of donations with donor restrictions.
