Recognized by erect to expanded leaves, oblong to oblong-lanceolate, strongly
concave, apex long acuminate, apical cells longer, alar and supra-alar
cells larger. Our samples were found in rocks near to
the stream. Terricolous, corticolous and rupicolous in Amazon Forest, Atlantic
Forest and Cerrado, recorded in the states of BA, DF,
ES, GO, MG, MT, MS, PA, PE, PR, RJ, RO,
RR, RS, SC, SP and TO. Brazilian endemic. Despite the
