The legitimizing myths of racist ideologies are explicit -or covertly

throughout the individual life cycle and in the succession of
generations, to the confinement of black people in general, and

social structure (Osorio, 2004). It means that black Brazilian women
are in a cumulative disadvantages cycle (Hasenbalg, 2005) throughout the
