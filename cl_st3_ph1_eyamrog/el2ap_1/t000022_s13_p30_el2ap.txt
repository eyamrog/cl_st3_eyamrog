Outside of the journals of Brazil, Mexico, Puerto Rico and
Chile, an average of 5.4% of the articles are in
English. On the other hand, Portuguese as a language of
publication outside of the journals of Brazil journals is very
low, reaching 5.4% of the articles in the journals of
Costa Rica, 3.8% in journals edited in Uruguay, and 2.5%
in Ecuador. Conversely, in the Spanish-speaking area, Spanish is by
far the predominant language. In journal articles from Bolivia, Cuba,
Ecuador, Peru, Dominican Republic, Uruguay and Venezuela, Spanish accounts for
more than 90%.
