Rhizomes short to long-creeping, glabrous or with catenate hairs. Leaves
large, to 4 m long; laminae 2-4-pinnate; veins free; indument
formed by catenate hairs, acicular hairs, or absent; sori marginal,
discrete, at the tip of single veins; inner and outer
indusia fused into purse- or cup-shaped or hemi-globose indusia (Smith
et al. 2006).
