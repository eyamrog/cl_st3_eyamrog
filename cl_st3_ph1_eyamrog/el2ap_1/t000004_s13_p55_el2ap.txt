Sandro explained that the movements should be fluid, almost as
if when you are drunk. “The old mestres would sometimes
pretend that they are drunk, that they are harmless. People
would come to them and say – come and play
with me, I’ll give you money! So they took the
money and then destroyed them.” Sandro was staggering around the
room, all of a sudden performing a few highly precise
attacks. (Fieldnotes)
