For articles in journals edited in Brazil (n=396,293), Portuguese has
the highest percentage in the Social Sciences and in Multidisciplinary-SS&H
(85%). Articles in English are the majority in two disciplinary
areas, namely Engineering and Technology (65.1%) and Natural and Exact
Sciences (54.5%). The percentages are also high for Medical and
Health Sciences (44.4%) and Multidisciplinary (42.4%). Spanish has a stronger
presence than Portuguese in the journals of the group of
Spanish-speaking countries. Consequently, 8.4% of the articles in the Multidisciplinary-SSH
journals and 7.9% of those in the Humanities are published
in Spanish.
