The contramestre, the jogadores, the songs and sayings, the lexical
framing of the event, involving shared terminology for the different
elements of the capoeira assemblage, drawn from Afro-Brazilian Portuguese, the
roda, the berimbau and other instruments all come together in
performance which resonates with deep cultural memory. In doing so
they are bringing something together in an agencement/assemblage, both a
repetition and a novelty: the regulated improvisations of practice.
