Study area – The Parque Estadual das Fontes do Ipiranga
(PEFI) is located in the municipality of São Paulo at
coordinates 23°39’S and 46°37’W, with an average altitude of 798
m (Melhem et al. 1981). The predominant vegetation type in
the area is Dense Ombrophilous Rainforest. Sampling units were selected
near Lago das Garças, along a dirt road that connects
the Instituto de Botânica to the Zoológico de São Paulo,
where H. coronarium is widely distributed (Figure 1).
