When asked to select the primary reason from four options
to explain their change in opinion, 53% of participants whose
perception of scientists had improved chose “because they are people
who provide great discoveries to society” as the main reason.
Among those whose opinion of scientists had worsened, 43% selected
“other” as their primary reason, while 33% chose “they only
discover things and develop products to earn money” as the
second most common response.
