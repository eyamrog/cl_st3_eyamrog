To achieve the stated objective, this article will proceed as
follows: first, it will present the construction of the concept
of mimesis in the works of the selected authors; second,
it will justify the applicability of this concept to University
Social Responsibility (USR) in the context of moral education within
research; third, it will detail the methodological choices adopted; fourth,
it will analyze the opinions of undergraduate students who have
participated in USR activities, based on responses to an online
questionnaire specifically designed for this study; and finally, it will
present the conclusions.
