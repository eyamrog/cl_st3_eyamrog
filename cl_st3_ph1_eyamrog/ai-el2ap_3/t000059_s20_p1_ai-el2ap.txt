The search identified 148,667 articles distributed across a minimum of
933 journals, although some entries lacked an ISSN. Of these,
130,312 articles were published in 698 journals that were systematically
indexed by LILACS during the study period. Ultimately, 126,774 of
these were signed articles published in 568 journals, each of
which contained at least 50 signed articles between 2015 and
2019.
