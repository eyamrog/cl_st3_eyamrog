Only 13 of the 59 plant families identified in the
study area were present at all four sites; these included
Apiaceae, Asteraceae, Boraginaceae, Brassicaceae, Calyceraceae, Caryophyllaceae, Chenopodiaceae, Euphorbiaceae, Fabaceae, Plantaginaceae,
Poaceae, Solanaceae, Verbenaceae, and Zygophyllaceae. Several other families were found
at three of the four sites. Specifically, the sites PP,
FE, and VP (excluding RZ) shared 13 families, while the
sites FE, VP, and RZ (excluding PP) shared 7 families.
