Non-academic readings were less common in the syllabuses analyzed. Specifically,
873 media pieces were included in 194 syllabuses, representing 5.7%
of all syllabuses, with an average of 4.5 media items
per syllabus. Additionally, 918 trade book chapters were distributed across
148 syllabuses, accounting for 4.4% of syllabuses, with an average
of 6.2 chapters per syllabus containing trade book chapters. There
was substantial overlap in reading types, as indicated by the
total number of syllabuses with readings (923) compared to the
number of syllabuses containing each reading type, suggesting that most
syllabuses with readings included more than one type of material.
