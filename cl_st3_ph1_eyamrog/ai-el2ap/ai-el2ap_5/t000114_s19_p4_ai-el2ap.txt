Stem leaves are ovate with an acuminate apex and serrate
margins; the costae are strong. Branch leaves are distant, erect
to widely spreading, and range from lanceolate to ovate in
shape, with apices that are acute to shortly acuminate. This
species is corticolous, rupicolous, and terricolous within the Atlantic Forest,
and has been recorded in the states of Espírito Santo,
Rio de Janeiro, Paraná, Rio Grande do Sul, Santa Catarina,
and São Paulo, as well as throughout the Neotropical region.
