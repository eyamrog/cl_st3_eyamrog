The study received approval from the National Research Ethics Committee
(CONEP), under registration number 1.756.060/2016, and participation was contingent upon
the signing of the Free and Informed Consent Form (FICF)

local indigenous leaders and the Litoral Sul Special Indigenous Sanitary
District (DSEI), which has jurisdiction over the indigenous village where
the study was conducted.
