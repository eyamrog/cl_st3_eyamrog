According to the Köppen classification, the climate is classified as
Aw (humid tropical), characterized by heavy rainfall during the austral
summer and relatively dry conditions in the winter. Mean monthly
temperatures vary from 25°C in February to 19°C in June,
with an annual average of approximately 22°C. Annual precipitation generally
ranges from 2,000 to 2,500 mm, but can reach up
to 3,300 mm in years with exceptionally high rainfall (Coelho
Netto 2005; ICMBIO 2008).
