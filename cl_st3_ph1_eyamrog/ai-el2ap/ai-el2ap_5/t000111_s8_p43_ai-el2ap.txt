In Brazilian Portuguese, the analysis of relative clauses varies, with
some researchers proposing that they consistently involve wh-movement (Kato &
Nunes, 2009), while others argue that wh-movement is not always
present (Tarallo, 1984; Kennedy, 2007; Medeiros Junior, 2020). Despite this
lack of consensus, which will not be addressed in this
paper, it is generally observed that the relative complementizer phrase
(CP) in Portuguese is typically introduced by the element que.
