The standardized average mortality rate for femicide in the municipalities
of Sergipe was 26.80 deaths per 100,000 women. The spatial
distribution of femicide rates is presented in Figure 1. The
highest rates were observed in the regions of Grande Aracaju
(45.54 deaths per 100,000 women), Sul (32.97), Agreste Central (32.44),
Médio Sertão (27.37), Centro Sul (26.39), Baixo São Francisco (17.59),
Alto Sertão (16.79), and Leste (16.04), in descending order. Among
the 75 municipalities in the state, the capital, Aracaju, ranked
37th in terms of femicide rates.
