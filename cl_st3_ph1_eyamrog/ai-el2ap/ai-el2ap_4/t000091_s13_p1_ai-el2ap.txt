Herbal medicines exhibiting anxiolytic and hypnotic effects have attracted increasing
attention across various medical disciplines, primarily due to their relatively
low incidence of adverse effects (Farah et al., 2019). Studies
in plant-based psychopharmacology have identified several promising compounds that may
offer therapeutic benefits for the management of general anxiety as
well as specific anxiety disorders (Sarris et al., 2013; Liu
et al., 2015).
