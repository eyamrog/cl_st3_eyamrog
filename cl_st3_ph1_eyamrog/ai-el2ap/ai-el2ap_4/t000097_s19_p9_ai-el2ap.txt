Of the studies included, 15 incorporated a control group. The
control group interventions varied and included usual or routine care,
general health advice and guidelines for weight reduction, provision of
a device to monitor physical activity and exercise, active control
interventions such as CoordiFit, guidance on physical exercises for postoperative
recovery and breast cancer treatment, supervised myofascial release training three
times per week, and recommendations for performing aerobic activity and
achieving a minimum of 10,000 steps per day.
