The crude death rate (Table 2) among hospitalized COVID-19 patients
was higher in the state of Rio de Janeiro than
in São Paulo across all age groups and in both
private and public hospitals. According to Table 2, the crude
risk of mortality for patients aged 18 to 40 years
was 2.9 times higher in private hospitals and 2.1 times
higher in public hospitals in Rio de Janeiro compared to
São Paulo. For patients aged 90 years or older, these
values were 1.6 and 1.4, respectively.
