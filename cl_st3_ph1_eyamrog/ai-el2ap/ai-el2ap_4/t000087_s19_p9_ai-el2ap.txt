RNA extracted from a positive qPCR test of one symptomatic,
fully vaccinated individual was sequenced as described in the Methods
section. The resulting genome exhibited 99.48% coverage and was classified
as the Delta variant of concern (B.1.617.2 lineage) using the
Pangolin COVID-19 Lineage Assigner. The sequence has been submitted to
GISAID under Submission Number EPI_ISL_3982391.
