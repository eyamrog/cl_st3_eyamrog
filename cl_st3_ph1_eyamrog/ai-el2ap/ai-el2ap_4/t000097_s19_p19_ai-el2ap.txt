The physical outcomes assessed in the protocol studies are presented
in Figure 2. Specifically, these outcomes include physical fitness, physical

services, biological assessments, neurotrophic levels, cardiac reserve, cardiac structure and
function, central vascular stiffness, blood pressure, sedentary behavior, side effects
related to cancer, and immune function, as referenced in the
corresponding studies.
