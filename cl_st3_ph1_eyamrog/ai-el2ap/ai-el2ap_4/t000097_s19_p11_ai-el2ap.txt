The physical activity and exercise interventions described in the study
protocols demonstrate considerable variability, including home-based walking programs, walking combined
with supervised exercise sessions, aerobic and strength or endurance training,
eurythmy and coordfit therapy, Pilates, belly dance, supervised exercise, exercise
guided by applications or internet software, activity monitoring using wearable
devices, internet-based interventions combined with Qigong, self-directed exercise, rehabilitation exercises,
high-intensity interval training (HIIT), personalized exercise regimens paired with dietary
programs, as well as interventions incorporating motivational meetings, dietary guidance,
and activity monitoring through wearable devices.
