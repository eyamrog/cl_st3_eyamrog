The study participants included women diagnosed with breast cancer, metastatic
breast cancer, overweight or obese women with breast cancer, and
elderly women with breast cancer. The age criteria for inclusion
varied across studies, with participants being over 18 years old
in some studies, between 18 and 65 years, 18 and
75 years, 19 and 70 years, 20 and 59 years,
30 and 75 years, 40 and 75 years, and between
65 and 70 years in others.
