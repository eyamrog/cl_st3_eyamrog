Data for the nine additional countries in this group—South Africa,
Argentina, Brazil, Colombia, the Philippines, Hungary, Mexico, Peru, and Poland—were
obtained from the official websites of their respective health authorities.
Accessing relevant information frequently necessitated the use of search terms
in the native languages of each country, as well as
the translation of table and graph headings into English. Consistent
with the methodology employed in previous studies [PECKHAM et al.
2020: 5], Google Translator was utilized to facilitate these translations.
