Table 1 presents all the indicators utilized by U-Multirank, categorized
according to the five dimensions. For each indicator, the table
provides a description, the quantity of available data, unavailable data,
and data classified as "not applicable." Additionally, the data sources
are specified and divided into two categories: "IQ," referring to
data collected through questionnaires completed by the institutions, and "IND,"
indicating data obtained from independent sources such as the Web
of Science.
