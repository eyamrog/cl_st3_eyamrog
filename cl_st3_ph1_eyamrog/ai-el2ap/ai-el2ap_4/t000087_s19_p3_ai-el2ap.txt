Following the event, 90.3% (n = 148) of participants underwent
COVID-19 testing; 33.6% received a PCR test, and 56.7% received
a rapid antigen test. A total of 41 individuals were
classified as COVID-19 positive: 35 had a positive test result,
while 6 either had a negative test result or were
not tested but reported six symptoms consistent with COVID-19, including
anosmia.
