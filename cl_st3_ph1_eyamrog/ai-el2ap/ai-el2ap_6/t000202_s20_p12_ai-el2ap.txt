A total of 61% of students reported regularly listening to
the radio, with 37% being female and 23% male. Among
these students, 28% listened to the radio daily, 26% at
least once a week, and 7% at least once a
month. Regarding the duration of radio listening from Monday to
Friday, 47% of university students spent between one and two
hours per day, 8% listened for three to four hours
per day, 4% for five to six hours per day,
and 2% for more than six hours per day. In
terms of program preferences, musical programs were the most frequently
listened to (27%), followed by news and information (12%), entertainment
programs (8%), music concerts (6%), sports (3%), cultural programs (1%),
and discussion panels (1%) (see Figure 5).
