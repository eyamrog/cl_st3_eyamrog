Eighty-two percent of university students utilize the technological resources provided
by the UNAE library, with 11% reporting daily use, 42%
using them at least once a week, 21% at least
once a month, and 8% at least once a year.
Additionally, 26% of university students access digital databases, with 3%
doing so daily, 9% at least once a week, 9%
at least once a month, and 5% at least once
a year.
