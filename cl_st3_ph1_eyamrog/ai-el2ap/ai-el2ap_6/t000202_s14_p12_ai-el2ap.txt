Regarding gender distribution, women constitute 63% of the sample, while
men represent 37%. Analysis of age groups indicates that slightly
more than one-quarter of the students are older than the
typical age range for university students in their respective academic
cycles. Specifically, 71% of students are between 18 and 22
years old, 22% are between 23 and 27 years old,
5% are between 28 and 32 years old, 1.4% are
between 33 and 47 years old, and 1% are older
than 38 years.
