According to data from the Johns Hopkins Coronavirus Resource Center,
as of the evening of May 31, 2020, there were
6,194,508 confirmed cases of COVID-19 and 372,501 confirmed deaths worldwide.
On that date, the countries with the highest numbers of
confirmed cases were the United States (1,790,191), Brazil (514,849), and
Russia (414,878). The highest numbers of confirmed deaths were reported
in the United States (104,383), the United Kingdom (38,571), and
Italy (33,415), with Brazil ranking fourth with 29,314 deaths.
