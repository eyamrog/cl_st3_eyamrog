Almost all students in the analyzed population possess a cell
phone (96%), and a substantial proportion own a smartphone (70%).
Analysis of expenditure on smart telephony indicates that a higher
percentage of female students (14.4%) invest between $200 and $299
in these devices, whereas a greater proportion of male students
(10.5%) spend between $100 and $199. In terms of monthly
cellular telephony costs, the majority of both male (35.2%) and
female (25.3%) students report expenses within the $0 to $19
range.
