This study is justified by its examination of the patterns
in scientific publications related to SARS-CoV-2, providing an additional resource
for the scientific community engaged in research on this significant
disease, which has resulted in substantial public health, economic, and
social impacts. The central research question is: What are the
characteristics of scientific production on SARS-CoV-2 within the Web of
Science database? To address this question, the study aims to
describe and analyze the publication trends of scientific articles on
SARS-CoV-2 available in the Web of Science, utilizing relevant indicators
and metrics.
