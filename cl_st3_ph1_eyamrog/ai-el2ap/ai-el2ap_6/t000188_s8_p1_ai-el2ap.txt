The global spread of the disease caused by the coronavirus
SARS-CoV-2, known as COVID-19, has resulted in a significant public
health emergency. On January 30, 2020, the World Health Organization
(WHO) classified the outbreak as a Public Health Emergency of
International Concern, which represents the highest alert level under the
International Health Regulations. Subsequently, on March 11, 2020, the WHO
officially characterized the situation as a pandemic.
