The coronavirus disease (COVID-19) pandemic originated in Wuhan, China, in
late 2019 and had spread to multiple countries by early
2020. Subsequently, the number of cases increased rapidly on a
global scale. As of 19 June 2020, Brazil was among
the most affected countries, with confirmed cases reported in all
five regions. At the onset of this study, Brazil had
recorded 1,038,568 confirmed COVID-19 cases, including 49,090 deaths and 520,360
recoveries.
