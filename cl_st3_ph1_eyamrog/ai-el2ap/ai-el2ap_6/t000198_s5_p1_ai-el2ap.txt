At the time of writing (mid-June 2020), countries were experiencing
varying stages of the COVID-19 epidemic. Brazil was approaching the
peak of its epidemic curve, the United States was observing
a decline in case numbers, and Australia had largely flattened
its curve, with new cases nearing elimination. Given these differing
epidemiological contexts, it is pertinent to compare the strategies each
country is employing to plan and implement the resumption of
clinical teaching in dentistry.
