A total of 33 taxa of aquatic Hyphomycetes were identified
in the water bodies of Parque Estadual da Ilha do
Cardoso, with 97 occurrences documented (Table 2). During the first
sampling, 18 taxa were recorded, corresponding to 26 occurrences. The
second and third samplings yielded 15 and 14 taxa, with
22 and 19 occurrences, respectively. In the fourth sampling, 17
taxa were observed, accounting for 30 occurrences.
