This electronic survey was conducted online from April 6 to
May 10, 2020, utilizing formr version 0.18.3 (Arslan & Tata,
2018), an open-source survey framework (Arslan et al., 2019). The
complete formr run, including all questionnaires and their interconnections, is
publicly accessible via the Open Science Framework (Fontenelle & Sarti,
2020a). Participants were drawn from four distinct groups of RBMFC
stakeholders.
