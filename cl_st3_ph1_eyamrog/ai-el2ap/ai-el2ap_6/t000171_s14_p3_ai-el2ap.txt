Data on the number of rapid tests performed per 1,000
inhabitants between March 4 and June 22, 2020, were obtained
from the e-SUS Epidemiological Surveillance platform (e-SUS VE) via the
DATASUS system. Similarly, the number of RT-PCR tests conducted between
March 4, 2020, and June 21, 2020, was extracted from
the Laboratory Environment Manager platform.
