Site 1 (Perequê Waterfall) and Site 5 (Perequê River) were
sampled during all field trips, which took place in June,
August, and November 2012, as well as February 2013. Site
2 (Cor de Chá River) had sufficient water flow to
allow sampling only in February 2013. Site 3 (Córrego) was
visited during the first three field trips, while Site 4
(Poço das Antas) was sampled only during the last two
trips, in November 2012 and February 2013.
