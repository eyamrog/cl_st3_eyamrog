Of the university students analyzed, 63% reported reading newspapers or
magazines, with 42% of women and 21% of men indicating
this behavior. Regarding the frequency of reading general information press,
whether paid or freely available online, 13% of students read
daily, 15% read only on weekends, 19% read at least
once a week, 9% read at least once a month,
4% read at least once a quarter, 3% read at
least once a year, and 2% reported never or almost
never reading such materials.
