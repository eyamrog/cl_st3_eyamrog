During this period, the state of Amazonas reported the highest
cumulative rate of COVID-19 deaths per 1,000,000 inhabitants (321.14), followed
by Ceará (161.63), Pernambuco (144.50), Rio de Janeiro (141.21), and
Pará (133.10). In contrast, the lowest cumulative rates were observed
in Mato Grosso do Sul (5.04), Minas Gerais (6.90), Mato
Grosso (7.46), and Goiás (9.55).
