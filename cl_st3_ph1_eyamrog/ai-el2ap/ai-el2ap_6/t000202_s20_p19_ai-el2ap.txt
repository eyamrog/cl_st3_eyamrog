All university students in the sample reported watching videos, with
63% of viewers being women and 37% men, and 77%
indicating daily viewing frequency. Regarding content preferences, 31% primarily watch
musicals, 23% watch series, 15% watch documentaries, and 5% watch
educational programs. The most commonly used devices for viewing are
cell phones (47%), followed by computers (42%) and tablets (3%).
In terms of video sources, 50% of students obtain videos
illegally from the internet, 6% use streaming services, and 5%
acquire them from video stores.
