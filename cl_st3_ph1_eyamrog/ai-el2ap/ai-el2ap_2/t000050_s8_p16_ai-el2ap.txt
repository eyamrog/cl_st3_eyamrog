A total of 3,218 specimens were identified to either the
genus or species level. The most abundant genera were Trichophoromyia
(20%), Nyssomyia (13%), and Psathyromyia (11%). The most abundant species
were Trichophoromyia brachipyga (14%), followed by Psathyromyia dreisbachi (9%) and
Nyssomyia pajoti (6%).
