Pseudostem 1–2 m tall. Leaves with striated sheaths; ligule truncate,
0.1–0.25 cm long, brown; leaves subsessile; pulvinus absent; blade narrowly
elliptic, 13–54.4 × 3.5–12.5 cm; apex acuminate, base cuneate, both
surfaces glabrous. Inflorescence a basal raceme on aphyllous scapes, 3–7.2
× 3.5–8.5 cm; scape 8.5–37.4 cm long, pink. Flowers with
pink pedicel, 0.5–1.5 cm long; bracts narrowly elliptic-obovate, 1.5–8 ×
1–2.5 cm, pink; bracteole 1.5–2.3 × 1–1.5 cm; calyx 1–2
× 1–1.9 cm, pinkish-red; corolla tube 0.6–1.4 cm, lobes narrowly
elliptic, 0.8–1.1 cm long, 3-lobed, white; labellum 3-lobed, slightly pink,
with pubescent base, 0.6–1.2 cm long; lateral lobes involute, rounded;
middle lobe entire or bilobulate, 1–1.5 cm long; lateral staminodes
linear, approximately 0.3 cm long; claw approximately 0.1 cm long;
anther 0.6–0.8 × 0.3–0.4 cm; filament approximately 0.2 cm long;
style 1.7–2 cm long, red; stigma 0.2–0.3 cm wide, cordiform,
puberulous, red; nectaries approximately 0.3 × 0.3 cm; ovary 1–1.3
× 0.5–0.7 cm, glabrous, red, with multiple ovules per locule.
Capsule 1–2 × 1.2–2 cm, dark red, turning black at
maturity. Seeds 0.3–0.5 cm long, bright brown, with orange aril.
