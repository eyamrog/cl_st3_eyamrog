Table 1 presents the descriptive analysis of the sociodemographic and
clinical characteristics of the sample. Among the 286 participants, 77.9%
were female, with a mean age of 23.3 years (SD
= 4.9). The majority were veterans (87.9%), enrolled in the
Medicine program (44.2%), self-identified as white (49.8%), were single (85.8%),
attended private universities (80.4%), and primarily resided in the state
of Sergipe (58.2%).
