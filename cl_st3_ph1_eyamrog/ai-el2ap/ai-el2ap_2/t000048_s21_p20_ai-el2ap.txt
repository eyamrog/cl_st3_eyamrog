Hedychium coccineum is the only species of Zingiberales present in
the Núcleo Curucutu that is cultivated in Brazil. Its native
distribution includes several Asian countries, such as Bhutan, India, Laos,
Myanmar, Nepal, Sri Lanka, and Thailand (Wongsuwan & Picheansoonthon 2011).
In Brazil, the species has been recorded in the states
of Rio de Janeiro, São Paulo, Paraná, Santa Catarina, and
possibly Pernambuco (André 2020c). Additionally, its occurrence has been documented
in the Distrito Federal and Rio Grande do Sul (Pereira
B.A.S. 163; Baptista L. 673).
