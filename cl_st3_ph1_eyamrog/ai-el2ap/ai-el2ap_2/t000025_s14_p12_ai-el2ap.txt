The databases and sources consulted for this research included CINAHL
(EBSCO), LILACS (VHL Regional Portal), Embase, Scopus, Web of Science,
Medline (PubMed), Cochrane, NICE, and JBISRIR. Additionally, unpublished studies were
identified through the Open Access Scientific Repository (Canada), the Canadian
Dissertation and Thesis Portal, the Tesis Doctorales Database - Teseo
(Spain), the CAPES Thesis Bank (Brazil), Google Scholar (including textbooks
and congress proceedings), and the European Thesis and Dissertation Database
(DART-Europe).
