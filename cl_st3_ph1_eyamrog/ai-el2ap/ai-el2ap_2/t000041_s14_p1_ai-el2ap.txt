Study area – The municipality of Passagem is situated in
the mesoregion of the Paraíba hinterland, bordering Quixaba and São
Mamede to the north, Taperoá and Cacimba to the south,
and Cacimba de Areia to the east (IBGE 2019) (Figure
1). The region has a semi-arid (Bsh) climate, characterized as
warm and dry, with an average annual temperature of 25°C
(Francisco 2015; IBGE 2019).
