Instead of utilizing images and fill-in-the-blank exercises, the EAL teacher
may instruct students to research a career of interest using
online or print resources. This research can be conducted at
home with support from parents, relatives, or friends, and the
findings are then presented to classmates during class. In a
group setting, each student provides a brief description of their
chosen career, while classmates attempt to identify the occupation being
described. The activity concludes with the student confirming the correct
answer by completing the sentence, “I want to be a…”.
