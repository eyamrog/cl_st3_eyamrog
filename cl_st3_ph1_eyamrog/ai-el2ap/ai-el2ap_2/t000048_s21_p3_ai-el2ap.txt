Goeppertia monophylla is endemic to Brazil and occurs in the
Atlantic Forest within the states of Bahia, Espírito Santo, Minas
Gerais, Rio de Janeiro, São Paulo, Paraná, Santa Catarina, and
Rio Grande do Sul (Saka et al. 2020). In the
Núcleo Curucutu, this species is primarily found in humid, shaded
habitats in proximity to watercourses.
