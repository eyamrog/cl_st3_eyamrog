The 'promotion from within' approach (Bondioli, 2008; Bondioli & Savio,
2009; Bondioli, 2015a, 2015b; Savio, 2003; Bondioli & Savio, 2012;
Savio, 2013) was adopted to ensure alignment with the aforementioned
theories and sources. This approach is integrated into the established
conceptual framework and is characterized by a participatory strategy, as
the planning of educational activities is informed by children's curiosity
and incorporates their input.
