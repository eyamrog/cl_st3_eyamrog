FIOCRUZ/COLFLEB houses 922 type specimens representing 151 species, encompassing holotypes,
allotypes, paratypes, plesiotypes, cotypes, topotypes, homeotypes, syntypes, and neotypes. The
collection also includes a diverse assemblage of fossil Neotropical sand
fly species, comprising 47 amber samples from the Dominican Republic
that collectively preserve 162 sand flies belonging to 9 species.
In addition, FIOCRUZ/COLFLEB maintains over 700 voucher specimens that have
been deposited as part of ongoing DNA barcoding studies.
