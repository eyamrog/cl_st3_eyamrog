The majority of respondents (91.4%) selected a positive term to
describe their perception of science, with "knowledge" being the most
frequently chosen (44.0%), followed by "progress" (28.7%). A higher proportion
of positive perceptions was observed among male respondents (94.2%), students
attending private schools (94.5%), those enrolled in high school (92.0%),
and individuals with at least one parent holding a university
degree (92.8%).
