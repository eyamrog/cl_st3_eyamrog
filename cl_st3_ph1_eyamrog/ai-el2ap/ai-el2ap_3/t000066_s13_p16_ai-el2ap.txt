The poster (Figure 1) lists the names of the three
professors who organized the workshop and served as facilitators, as
well as the names of the professionals they invited to
participate as guests. A total of eight professionals attended the
workshop, residing in Brazil, the USA, and Ireland at the
time. Among the participants, one was Kenyan, three were Brazilians
living abroad, and four were Brazilians from various regions of
Brazil. Due to the international scope of the workshop, which
was open to anyone who registered through the GPB website,
English was selected as the language of communication. Several of
the workshop activities are described and analyzed in the following
sections.
