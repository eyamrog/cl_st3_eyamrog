The data collection instrument, an online questionnaire initially developed in
2016, has undergone minor modifications to accommodate the specific target
audiences of the various research phases. The study is registered
with the Scientific Committee of (omitted to preserve anonymity) and
adheres to all relevant research ethics protocols. In the version
employed for the current phase, the questionnaire comprised eight closed-ended
questions, thirteen open-ended questions, and one item utilizing the Likert
Intensity Scale. To minimize the time required for completion, certain
questions related to respondent profile characterization that were included in
previous phases were omitted in this iteration.
