FAYS encompasses approximately 2,608 hectares, comprising forest fragments of lowland,
Brazilian savanna (cerrado), semideciduous forest, riparian forest, and plantations of
Eucalyptus spp. and Pinus spp. The climate is classified as
Cwa according to Rolim et al. (2007), characterized by hot,
rainy summers and dry winters. In 2018, the mean annual
temperature was 21.90 °C, and the total annual precipitation was
1,291 mm (Universidade de São Paulo 2020).
