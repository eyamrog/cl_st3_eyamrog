When participants were asked about their reasons for wanting to
study science-related fields (including natural sciences, mathematics, statistics, or health
sciences), the most frequently selected reason among those intending to
pursue health sciences was an interest in the subject matter,
with 39.7% indicating “I like that type of knowledge.” Similarly,
48.4% of respondents interested in natural sciences, mathematics, and statistics
selected this reason. Additionally, 3.2% of individuals planning to enter
these fields reported that the ongoing pandemic had influenced their
decision.
