Since 2010, the metadata of biological specimens housed in FIOCRUZ/COLFLEB
have been digitized, and, among the various biological collections at
Fiocruz [15], this collection has the highest volume of data
published online. The online catalogue is also integrated with the
speciesLink network (<http://www.splink.org.br/search?collectioncode=FIOCRUZ-COLFLEB&group=animais&lang=pt&action=openform>) and the Sistema de Informação sobre a
Biodiversidade Brasileira (SiBBr) (<http://ipt.fiocruz.br/ipt/resource?r=fiocruz_colfleb>).
