Identification. This species is characterized by its small basidiomata, measuring
up to 17 mm in width, an exoperidium covered with
short hairs up to 1 mm in length, a fibrillose
peristome, and basidiospores measuring 4.4–6 × 4.3–5.9 (occasionally up to
6.8) μm in diameter (Crous et al. 2016; Lima &
Baseia 2018).
