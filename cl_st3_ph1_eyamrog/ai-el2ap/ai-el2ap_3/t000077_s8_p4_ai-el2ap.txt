Regarding the effects on the labor market, evidence suggests that
they are likely to be significant and persistent (Campello et
al., 2020). A prominent area of research has focused on
the unequal distribution of the negative impacts associated with the
Coronavirus pandemic, with a growing consensus that these effects are
not uniform and have the potential to exacerbate existing inequalities
(Adams-Prassl et al., 2020; Bartik et al., 2020; Brussevich et
al., 2020; Crowley & Doran, 2020; Gallacher & Hossain, 2020;
Gaudecker et al., 2020; Montenovo et al., 2020; Yasenov, 2020).
