The state of Paraná is located in the subtropical region
of South America, in southern Brazil, between the coordinates 22°30’44”
S to 26°43’08” S and 48°00’11” W to 54°36’32” W,
and encompasses a territorial area of 199,298 km² [44]. It
comprises five phytogeographic regions: Seasonal Semideciduous Forest, Mixed Ombrophilous Forest,
Dense Ombrophilous Forest, Steppe, and Savanna [45,46]. According to the
Köppen climate classification, Paraná exhibits both subtropical and tropical climates,
with an average annual temperature of 19°C, ranging from 12°C
to 25.9°C, and an average annual precipitation of 1,300 mm
[47].
