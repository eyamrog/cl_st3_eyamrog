In response to the pandemic, the Brazilian Institute of Geography
and Statistics (IBGE) conducted a supplementary survey, PNAD COVID-19, which
commenced on May 4, 2020. Data collection for PNAD COVID-19
was performed via telephone interviews in approximately 193,000 households throughout
the national territory. The survey instrument consisted of two sections:
one addressing health-related topics and the other focusing on labor-related
issues.
