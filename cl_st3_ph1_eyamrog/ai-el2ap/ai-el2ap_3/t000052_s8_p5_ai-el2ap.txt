This study provides the first description of Bagrobdella species from
Lake Tanganyika. Previously, four species within this genus have been
described from various African countries: Bagrobdella auchenoglanii Paperna, 1969, B.
fraudulenta Euzet & Le Brun, 1990, and B. anthopenis Euzet
& Le Brun, 1990, all from Auchenoglanis occidentalis (Valenciennes, 1840)
in Ghana, Uganda, and Mali (Euzet & Le Brun 1990),
and B. parauchenoglanis Akoumba, Pariselle, Tombi & Bilong Bilong, 2017
from Parauchenoglanis monkei (Keilhack, 1910) in Cameroon (Akoumba et al.
2017).
