A total of 22 articles and books published between 1964
and 2022 were reviewed to obtain the data. In total,
1,096 observations, including two from preserved specimens, were recorded for
the state of Acre. These records were obtained from eight
municipalities: Acrelândia, Assis Brasil, Brasiléia, Bujari, Cruzeiro do Sul, Feijó,
Rio Branco, and Xapuri. Across these localities, 116 species belonging
to 15 genera of sand flies were identified. Consequently, based
on all available studies on phlebotomine fauna, 116 sand fly
species have been reported in the state of Acre.
