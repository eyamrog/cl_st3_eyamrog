For this stage, the standard strains utilized were Streptococcus mutans
(ATCC 25175), Streptococcus oralis (ATCC 10557), Streptococcus mitis (ATCC 903),
and Streptococcus salivarius (ATCC 7073), all obtained from the American
Type Culture Collection (ATCC) and supplied by the Oswaldo Cruz
Foundation (FIOCRUZ-RJ).
