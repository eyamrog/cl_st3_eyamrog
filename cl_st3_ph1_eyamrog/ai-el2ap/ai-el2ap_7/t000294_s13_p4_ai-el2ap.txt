Given the potential for the virus to remain viable in
the environment for several days, it is necessary to clean
facilities and areas that may be contaminated with SARS-CoV-2 prior
to reuse, utilizing products that contain antimicrobial agents demonstrated to
be effective against coronaviruses.13,17 Although specific evidence regarding the efficacy
of these measures against SARS-CoV-2 is limited, cleaning with water
and household detergents, as well as the use of common
disinfectant products, is considered adequate for general household cleaning.19-20
