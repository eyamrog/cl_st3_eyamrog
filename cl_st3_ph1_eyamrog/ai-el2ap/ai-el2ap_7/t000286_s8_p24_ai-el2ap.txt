Turkey's significant economic growth and the structural reforms implemented over
the past decade have attracted considerable attention from international investors.
According to the EY Attractiveness Survey Europe, Turkey ranked as
the seventh most popular foreign direct investment (FDI) destination in
Europe in 2018. In that year, the country hosted 261
FDI projects, representing a 14 percent increase compared to the
previous year, and accounted for 4 percent of all FDI
projects in Europe.
