As previously mentioned, Latindex is a regional index that compiles
bibliographic data from 17 national resource centers located in Latin
America, the Caribbean, and the Iberian Peninsula. Among the 24,486
journals included in Latindex, 4,208 (17.2%) are classified as JUOJS.
Additionally, approximately two-thirds of JUOJS published within the Iberoamerican region
are indexed in Latindex.
