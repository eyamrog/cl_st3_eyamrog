(v) Motivation for publishing in high-impact journals in the field
is primarily driven by the recognition and validation that such
publications confer within the academic community. Publishing in these journals
is often associated with increased visibility, greater citation rates, and
enhanced professional reputation. Furthermore, successful publication in high-impact journals is
frequently considered a key criterion for career advancement, securing research
funding, and establishing collaborations. As a result, researchers are incentivized
to target these journals to maximize the dissemination and perceived
value of their work.
