The Leeds capoeira school Guardiões Brasileiros is affiliated with a
larger capoeira group based in the state of São Paulo,
Brazil. The school is characterized by significant diversity, including members
from Lusophone countries such as Portugal, Brazil, and Mozambique, as
well as from various European countries, including France and the
United Kingdom, and from non-Lusophone African countries. One of the
co-authors of this paper, Jolana, is a researcher from the
Czech Republic who is fluent in Portuguese and is herself
an apprentice capoeirista. This study focuses on the experiences of
the contramestre, Sandro, and Sophie, who is originally from France
but has resided in the United Kingdom for an extended
