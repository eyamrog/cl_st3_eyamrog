In journals published in countries where Spanish is the official
language, English accounts for 41.9% of articles in the Natural
and Exact Sciences and 28.4% in Engineering and Technology. Portuguese
is minimally represented, reaching a maximum of 2.9% only in
the Social Sciences. In contrast, Spanish is the predominant language
in the Medical and Health Sciences as well as in
all three categories of Social Sciences and Humanities, where it
constitutes more than 91% of published articles.
