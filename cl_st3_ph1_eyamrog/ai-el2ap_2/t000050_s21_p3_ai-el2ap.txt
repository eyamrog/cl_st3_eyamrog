According to data from the Indigenous Health Care Information System
(SIASI), managed by the Special Secretariat of Indigenous Health (SESAI),
the indigenous population of the Middle Purus region is estimated
at approximately 8,117 individuals. These individuals belong to 13 distinct
ethnic groups and are distributed across 24 demarcated indigenous territories
and an additional 6 non-demarcated areas, which are either unidentified
or not yet delimited. The total area of the demarcated
indigenous lands amounts to 189,870.964 hectares.
