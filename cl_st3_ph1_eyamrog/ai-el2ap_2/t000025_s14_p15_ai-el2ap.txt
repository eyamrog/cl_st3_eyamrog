Extracted data comprised information on the author(s), year of publication,
country of origin, study objectives, methods, topical therapies employed, and
results. Additionally, specific details were collected regarding tumor type and
stage, wound location and stage, pain assessment using standardized tools,
pain classification, pain location and duration, and the use of
medications for wound pain management. The authors of one included
article (Ciałkowska-Rysz & Dzierżanowski, 2019) were contacted to obtain supplementary
data. Consistent with the methodology of scoping reviews, a formal
critical appraisal of the included articles was not performed.
