Zingiberales is an order of tropical monocotyledonous plants comprising eight
families: Cannaceae Juss., with one genus and 10 species; Costaceae
Nakai, with six genera and 143 species; Heliconiaceae Nakai, with
one genus and 200 species; Lowiaceae Ridl., with one genus
and 20 species; Marantaceae R. Br., with 31 genera and
550 species; Musaceae Juss., with three genera and 91 species;
Strelitziaceae Hutch., with three genera and seven species; and Zingiberaceae
Martinov, with 56 genera and 1,600 species. In total, the
order includes 102 genera and 2,621 species (Stevens 2017).
