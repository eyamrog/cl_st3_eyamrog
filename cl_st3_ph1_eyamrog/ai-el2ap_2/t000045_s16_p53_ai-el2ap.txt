The individual submits the request to the councilor, who then
forwards the correspondence to our office. Although the request originates
from the individual, it is mediated by the councilor. We
receive a substantial volume of communications, including letters, emails, and
WhatsApp messages; the mayor provides our WhatsApp contact information, resulting
in a high frequency of incoming messages. Currently, the flow
of messages via WhatsApp is continuous (Chief of Staff 8).
