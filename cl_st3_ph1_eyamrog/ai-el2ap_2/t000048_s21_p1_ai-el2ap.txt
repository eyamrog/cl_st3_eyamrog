In the Núcleo Curucutu, four species of the order Zingiberales,
representing three different families, were recorded: Goeppertia monophylla (Vell.) Borchs.
& S.Suárez (Marantaceae), Heliconia farinosa Raddi (Heliconiaceae), and both Hedychium
coccineum Buch.-Ham. ex Sm. and Renealmia petasites Gagnep. (Zingiberaceae).
