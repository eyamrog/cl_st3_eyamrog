Herbs 1–1.5 m tall. Leaves with sheaths 21–37 cm long,
pubescent with denser trichomes along the margin and sericeous at
the base; ligule absent; petiole 13–18 cm long, puberulous; pulvinus
2–5 cm long, glabrous; blade 37–60 × 12–22 cm, elliptic,
with apex acute to acuminate, base slightly attenuated to acute,
and puberulous on the abaxial surface. Inflorescence terminal, simple, 6–10
× 2–4.5 cm, ellipsoid, green; internode below the last leaf
oblong or absent; peduncle 19–45.5 cm long, minutely pubescent; inflorescence
composed of 5 pairs of flowers; bracts 2–3 × 2–3
cm, monomorphic, obovate to broadly obovate, apex erect and rounded,
sometimes emarginate with a slightly wavy margin, green becoming dark
brown at the apex, puberulous, densely setose at the base,
occasionally with a darker and more undulate margin, tearing and
blackening with age; prophyll approximately 1.8 × 0.4 cm, elliptic
to ovate, 2–3-carenate, carinae membranous, sparsely puberulous; interphyll approximately 1.5
× 0.3 cm, elliptic to ovate, concave, membranous, sparsely puberulous;
bracteole approximately 1.8 × 0.4 cm, one per flower, asymmetrical,
lanceolate, carinate, glabrous, with a darker apex. Flowers approximately 3
cm long, grayish-white or pale yellow; calyx 1.8–2 × 0.4
cm, elliptic-lanceolate, concave, abaxial surface with minute trichomes, longer than
the corolla tube; corolla tube 2 cm long, lobes 0.8–1.1
cm long, elliptic; labellum absent; external staminode 0.7–0.8 cm long,
obovate, apex emarginate; stamen with lateral petaloid appendix approximately 0.4
× 0.2 cm; anther 0.2 cm long; style 0.8–1 cm
long, white; stigma 0.2 × 0.2 cm; ovary approximately 0.3
× 0.2 cm, globose, puberulous, grayish-white, with one ovule per
locule. Capsule 0.8–1.5 × 0.7–1 cm, bi- to trilocular, pink.
Seeds 0.7 × 0.4 cm, yellow, with a white aril.
