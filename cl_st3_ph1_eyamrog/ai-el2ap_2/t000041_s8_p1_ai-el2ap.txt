The Caatinga is situated in the northeastern semiarid region of
Brazil and covers an area of approximately 912,529 km² (Silva
et al. 2017), making it one of the most distinct
phytogeographic regions in the country. According to BFG (2021), the
flora of the Caatinga domain comprises 5,022 species, of which
2,307 species are recorded in the state of Paraíba.
