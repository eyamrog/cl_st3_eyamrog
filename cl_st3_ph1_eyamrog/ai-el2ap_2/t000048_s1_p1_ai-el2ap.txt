This study presents a taxonomic survey of Zingiberales Griseb. in
the Núcleo Curucutu, Parque Estadual Serra do Mar, São Paulo
State, Brazil. The research includes an identification key, detailed morphological
descriptions, illustrations, data on geographic distribution, and phenological information for
the species recorded. In the study area, the order Zingiberales
is represented by four species distributed across three families: Goeppertia
monophylla (Vell.) Borchs. & S.Suárez (Marantaceae), Heliconia farinosa Raddi (Heliconiaceae),
and Hedychium coccineum Buch.-Ham. ex Sm. and Renealmia petasites Gagnep.
(Zingiberaceae).
