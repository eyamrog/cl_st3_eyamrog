The study sample comprised 286 university students enrolled in 13
distinct undergraduate programs within the biological and health sciences, including
biology, biomedicine, physical education, nursing, pharmacy, physiotherapy, speech therapy, medicine,
nutrition, dentistry, psychology, chiropractic, and occupational therapy. Participants were recruited
through convenience sampling (nonprobability sampling) based on the following inclusion
criteria: (a) enrollment as an undergraduate student in a health-related
program at a Brazilian Higher Education Institution (HEI), (b) age
of 18 years or older, and (c) access to the
internet.
