Renealmia petasites is a species endemic to Brazil, primarily found
in the Atlantic Forest and distributed across the states of
Espírito Santo, Minas Gerais, Rio de Janeiro, São Paulo, Paraná,
and Santa Catarina (André 2020c). Additionally, its presence has been
documented in Bahia, based on specimens collected by L.A. Mattos
Silva (3673), M.M.M. Lopes (312), and D. Cardoso (1668).
