The modal density of the Oral-B advertisement contributes to the
construction of specific meaning potentials, as observed through several parameters.
The smiling behaviors of Social Actors 1, 2, and 3
are consistently evident across the campaign segments. These smiles are
intended to display the notably white color of the actors’
teeth to the viewers. The enhanced brightness of the teeth,
highlighted through the actors’ laughter, serves to demonstrate the purported
effects of the toothpaste on dental appearance over a given
period.
